
Stay in Touch with our Monday Report!
Dear Friend,
It is important to me that I'm able to be in touch with you about the
work I'm doing for the great people of Minnesota's 8th District.
However, unless you tell me that you'd like to keep receiving emails
from me, by law, I will be unable to email you from May 14th, 2014,
until after the general election in November.
To make sure you don't miss a Monday Report this summer and fall, all
you need to do is visit my website
or click the link below to enter your email
address.
We'll keep sending out our weekly newsletters to keep you updated on
legislation and issues important to you. I do hope you'll subscribe to
make sure you don't miss a thing.

to enter your email address and sign up for
weekly updates.
