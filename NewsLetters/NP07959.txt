Walberg Hosts Saline Roundtable and Votes for Bipartisan Job Training Reform Bill
Weekly Update
Hosted Chamber of Commerce roundtable in Saline where I gave an update on my work in D.C. to create jobs and grow the economy
Voted in favor of H.R. 803, the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act, a bipartisan bill to reform the nations job training system and help workers gain the education and skills they need
Helped pass H.R. 4718, legislation to make permanent a tax provision that would allow companies to deduct half the cost of new business investments
Questioned VA officials at an Oversight and Government Reform committee hearing about why senior executives received high performance ratings and bonuses in light of their management failures
Supported several pieces of bipartisan reform legislation in the Education and Workforce Committee to strengthen the higher education system
Sat down with Milans Mayor, Michael Armitage to discuss local issues affecting the people of Milan and across the state
