Congressman
Lamborn Hosts the 2015 Fifth Congressional District Art
Competition
3/16/15
Friday, March 20th at Library 21c,
Congressman Doug Lamborn will honor students from 20 high schools and home
schools throughout the Fifth Congressional District who participated in
the annual Congressional Art
Competition.
The competition has been held
every year since its creation in 1982 and provides Members of Congress
with a unique opportunity to showcase the creative talents of high
schoolers in their district. This nationwide event has seen over 700,000
students participate since 1982. The Grand Prize winner will receive an
Art Scholarship, roundtrip airfare to Washington DC, where their
painting will hang in the Capitol and the winner will be honored with a
reception at the White
House.
Please join the Congressman to help honor
and commend the exceptional young artists of the Fifth Congressional
District.
WHAT: Congressman Doug Lamborn honors local high school
students in the annual Fifth Congressional District Art
Competition
