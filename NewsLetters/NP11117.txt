
News

March 25, 2014
As part of a larger effort to improve government transparency and keep the constituents of Pennsylvania's 4th Congressional District informed of current legislative business, I send out weekly updates at the beginning of each week that Congress is in session. These updates are a brief overview of upcoming legislative matters to be addressed each week. Please click on my photo below to listen to the provided audio clip.
As always, if you have any other questions or concerns on any issue or need help with a federal agency, please contact one of my offices anytime.
CLICK ON PICTURE TO PLAY SHORT AUDIO
H.R. 1036
- A bill to designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 103 Center Street West in Eatonville, Washington, as the National Park Ranger Margaret Anderson Post Office (Sponsored by Rep. David Reichert / Oversight and Government Reform Committee)
H.R. 1228
- A bill to designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 123 South 9th Street in De Pere, Wisconsin, as the Corporal Justin D. Ross Post Office Building (Sponsored by Rep. Reid Ribble / Oversight and Government Reform Committee)
H.R. 3060
- A bill to designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 232 Southwest Johnson Avenue in Burleson, Texas, as the Sergeant William Moody Post Office Building (Sponsored by Rep. Roger Williams / Oversight and Government Reform Committee)
H.R. 2391
- A bill to designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 5323 Highway N in Cottleville, Missouri as the Lance Corporal Phillip Vinnedge Post Office (Sponsored by Rep. Ann Wagner / Oversight and Government Reform Committee)
H.R. 1451
- A bill to designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 14 Main Street in Brockport, New York, as the Staff Sergeant Nicholas J. Reid Post Office Building (Sponsored by Rep. Louis Slaughter / Oversight and Government Reform Committee)
H.R. 1376
- A bill to designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 369 Martin Luther King Jr. Drive in Jersey City, New Jersey, as the Judge Shirley A. Tolentino Post Office Building (Sponsored by Rep. Donald Payne / Oversight and Government Reform Committee)
H.R. 1813
- A bill to designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 162 Northeast Avenue in Tallmadge, Ohio, as the Lance Corporal Daniel Nathan Deyarmin Post Office Building (Sponsored by Rep. Tim Ryan / Oversight and Government Reform Committee)
H.R. 3771
- Philippines Charitable Giving Assistance Act - would accelerate the income tax benefits for charitable cash contributions for the relief of victims of the Typhoon Haiyan in the Philippines (Sponsored by Rep. Eric Swalwell / Ways &amp; Means Committee)
H.R. 4275
- Cooperative and Small Employer Charity Pension Flexibility Act - would amend the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 and the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to provide for cooperative and small employer charity pension plans (Sponsored by Rep. Susan Brooks / Education and the Workforce Committee)
H.R. 2824
- Preventing Government Waste and Protecting Coal Mining Jobs in America - would amend the Surface Mining Control and Reclamation Act of 1977 to stop the ongoing waste by the Department of the Interior of taxpayer resources and implement the final rule on excess spoil, mining waste, and buffers for perennial and intermittent streams (Sponsored by Rep. Bill Johnson / Natural Resources Committee)
H.R. 1459
- Ensuring Public Involvement in the Creation of Natural Monuments Act - would ensure that the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969 applies to the declaration of national monuments (Sponsored by Rep. Rob Bishop / Natural Resources Committee)
