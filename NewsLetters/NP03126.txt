Id like to invite you to join me for
my Farmers Advisory Board meeting on Thursday, February 19th at the Virginia Diner in Wakefield . On our agenda will be the farm bill, the impact federal regulations have on the agricultural industry, pending trade agreements, and any other issues affecting our farmers that you would like to discuss.
Congressman Randy Forbes Farmers Advisory Board Meeting
Thursday, February 19th
1:00PM - 2:30PM
The Virginia Diner
408 County Drive North, Wakefield
Feel free to order lunch from the Virginia Diner to enjoy during our discussion
My Farmers Advisory Board is made up of farmers who advise me on a wide range of issues impacting the agricultural community in the 4th Congressional District of Virginia. We use these meetings as working sessions where we share ideas and practical solutions to address current challenges. I hope you can join us and look forward to seeing you on the 19th.
To RSVP for this event and/or to join my Farmers Advisory Board, please contact Mr. Ron White, District Director at
Ronald.White@mail.house.gov ,
or (804) 318-1363.
