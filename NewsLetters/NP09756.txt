Congressman Gene Green's
12th Annual Job Fair
Monday, May 19, 2014
10:00 a.m. - 2:00 p.m.
San Jacinto College North-Monument Room
5800 Uvalde Road
Houston, Texas 77049
Congressman Green invites you to his 12th Annual Job Fair on Monday,
May 19th at San Jacinto College's North Campus. Companies will be
looking to fill full-time, part-time and summer positions during the
event. Participants should dress professionally, and be prepared with
resumes.
The job fair is being held in conjunction with Workforce
Solutions-North Shore and the North Channel Area Chamber of Commerce.
Representatives from 40 local businesses, across a variety of
industries, will be present and ready to hire, including: the City of
Houston, Boasso America Corporation, CVS-Caremark, Southwest Shipyard,
Harris County Sheriff's Office, Houston Police Department, Houston Fire
Department, Mission Foods, Gulf Stream Marine, Allied Barton, LG
Hospitality, United States Steel, Houston Area Plumbing Joint
Apprenticeship, Enterprise Products, Houston Independent School
District, Harris County Education Department, Hydrochem, YMCA, Harris
County Juvenile Probation and more.
Job openings range from customer service,
general labor, clerical, and
teaching to EMT/Paramedics, Security Officers, Juvenile Supervision
Officers, Plant Managers, and many more.
We hope to see everyone there!
Sincerely,
Gene Green
Member of Congress
