Dear Friend,
There is nothing more important than listening to the views of
Northeast Ohioans as I serve you in Congress. Hearing from you allows
me to do my job best, so I am asking that you please take this
opportunity to provide your feedback on what is most important to you
by answering the survey below.
Click HERE or on the image above to take this survey
We as a country are facing great challenges, and as your representative
in Congress it is critical that we have direct communication as we work
toward solutions for today's obstacles. As we work to achieve our
common goals of reining in federal spending and getting our economy
back on track,
please visit our website
to learn more about what I am
doing in Washington and Ohio and to stay connected with our office.
If you need any additional information, please visit our website at
renacci.house.gov
or call our Washington
office:(202) 225-3876
or Wadsworth
office:(330) 334-0040
.
