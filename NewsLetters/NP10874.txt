Dear Friend,
Reminder! We are only five days away from Women's Business Empowerment
Day on April 12 at CSU East Bay in Hayward. I'm thrilled we have such
an impressive line-up of speakers and over 100 people already
registered. But don't worry - you can still join us by RSVP-ing here:
http://womensbizday.eventbrite.com
This event was inspired by the principle that when women succeed,
America succeeds. This is the central tenet of my Women's Economic
Agenda, which aims to address the economic challenges facing women and
families today.
Our event is an effort to provide women of the East Bay with the tools
necessary to succeed in the workforce. The day will feature workshops
on a number of skills including:
*	How to start or expand your own small business
*	Perfecting the art of negotiation: salary, benefits, and
work/family balance
*	Achieving leadership within your company and community
To learn more and register please visit,
http://womensbizday.eventbrite.com
or contact my office.
It is an honor to serve as your representative in Congress. Please
contact my East Bay offices at 510-370-3322 or 925-460-5100 if you have
any further questions or if we can be of service in any way. To stay
connected, please follow me on Twitter
and join me on Facebook.
Sincerely,
