Dear
Friend,
The rain we saw in Maricopa County yesterday broke an
85-year record!
Because of the possible damage from
all of the flooding we've seen in the last few days, I'd like to provide
some information you might find useful:
o
If you live in a city, report your damage to
your business or home to your city officials.
Find who represents you at the city level by clicking on
the link for your city:
Phoenix ,
Scottsdale ,
Mesa ,
Fountain Hills ,
Cave Creek ,
Carefree ,
Paradise Valley ,
Fort McDowell ,
Glendale .
o
If you live in an unincorporated area of
Maricopa County, please report your damage to the Emergency
Management Office at 602-273-1411.
o
If you need shelter for the evening, please contact the Red
Cross at 1-800-842-7349. Temporary shelters have been set
up.
o
Please visit
www.fcd.maricopa.gov for up-to date storm information.
In closing, I
want to commend all of the emergency personnel who have responded
selflessly.
Thank you to everyone who has ensured the
safety of all of us here in
Arizona.
