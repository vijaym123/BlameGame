<![endif] This
afternoon, Rep. Miller met with the owners of Tubbs Brothers Ford Chrysler Dodge Jeep Ram and toured their Dealership located in Sandusky.
This year the Tubbs Brothers&#8217; Dealership celebrates their 60th year in business.
Marking the occasion, Rep. Miller
presented the owners with a special congressional recognition honoring their decades-long automotive sales and service to the surrounding area.
Rep. Miller also had the opportunity to discuss with the owners how their business models have developed over the years, including how their local business is affected
by the Affordable Care Act.
Pictured: Rep. Miller presenting certificate to three generations of Tubbs. Front: Rep. Miller and founder
Donald Tubbs. Back Row left to right: David Tubbs, Billie Prescott and Eric Tubbs.
