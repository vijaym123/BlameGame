Message from Representative Van Hollen
2014 Service Academy Forums
On April 24, 2014 and May 5, 2014 Congressman Van Hollen will host his
annual Service Academy forums with representatives from the U.S. Air
Force Academy, the U.S. Coast Guard Academy, the U.S. Merchant Marine
Academy, the U.S. Military Academy, and the U.S. Naval Academy for
classes entering July 2015. All interested 9th, 10th, and 11th grade
students and their parents are invited to attend to learn about the
academies and their admission requirements.
Note: RSVP's are appreciated, but not required.
Rockville Forum
Thursday, April 24, 2014
7 - 8:30 pm
(panel convenes at 7:15 pm)
Montgomery County Executive Office Building
101 Monroe Street
Rockville, MD 20850
Westminster Forum
Monday, May 5, 2014
7 - 8:30 pm
(panel convenes at 7:15 pm)
McDaniel College, Decker Forum (Building #13)
2 College Hill
Westminster, MD 21157
