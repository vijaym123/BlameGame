Every year, the U.S. House of Representatives
hosts the
Congressional Art
Competition . Students across the country compete to have their artwork
hang in the United States Capitol, and winners are invited to
Washington, DC for a reception in their honor.
Each Member of the
House gets to select one winning piece from a student in their
district, and that piece hangs in the Capitol for one year. Finding our
districts artwork is always a highlight when my office takes constituent
on
tours of the Capitol building . Im always
impressed by the incredible talents of the students in our
district.
Once again, I want YOU decide which students art will

you can vote on
my Facebook page
to choose the winner among four finalists.
