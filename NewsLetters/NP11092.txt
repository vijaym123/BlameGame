
A Message from Congressman Paul Broun
Broun to Host FREE Tax Prep Workshop
In conjunction with the Internal Revenue Service (IRS), Congressman Broun will host a FREE tax filing workshop from 1:00 pm - 4:00 pm
on Friday, April 4, 2014. The workshop will take place in the Multipurpose Room of the Athens-Clarke County Library, located at 2025 Baxter Street.
*The tax preparers at the workshops will not be able to assist businesses or individuals with complex forms.
Please visit my website to find out what individuals seeking tax preparation help must bring with them to the workshop.
2014 Veterans Fair
Representatives from Broun's office assist a local veteran
Thursday, March 20th, Congressman Broun hosted his annual Veterans Fair at the Georgia War Veterans Home. Attendees of this year's event were able to meet with representatives from the Atlanta VA Regional Office, Atlanta VA Medical Center, Carl Vinson VA Medical Center, Charlie Norwood VA Medical Center, Georgia War Veterans Home, and Georgia Department of Veterans Service. The representatives provided veterans with the opportunity to receive personal information and discover what benefits may be available to them.

Morgan County Student Appointed to West Point
Broun looks on as Riedlinger signs his certificate
To date, six students from Georgia's 10th Congressional District have received appointments to Service Academies. Dillon Riedlinger, of Rutledge, is the son of David and April Riedlinger. A senior at Morgan County High School, Riedlinger is the Student Body Vice President as well as a the captain of his schools soccer and cross country teams. Dillon has also served in Morgan County High Schools AFJROTC for the duration of grades 9-12.
**The locations for the 2014 Veterans Fair and the signing ceremony for Dillon Riedlinger were incorrect in Dr. Broun's previous newsletter. They have been corrected above.
