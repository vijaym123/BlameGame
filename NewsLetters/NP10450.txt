Hannah Wilson's winning artwork for 2013, "Shadow of Life," currently represents the Fourth District in our nation's capital.
Each spring, I am pleased to invite high school students to enter the
Congressional Art Competition , and theres still time to enter before the 5 p.m. deadline TOMORROW.
Open to all high school students living or attending school in the Fourth District of Oklahoma, the winning prize includes display of the artwork for one year in the United States Capitol and travel for the winner and a guest to Washington, DC for a reception, workshop and award presentation.
For more information about the contest and to download the official forms to enter,
visit my website
or call my Norman office at (405) 329-6500.
