Rep Walberg Hosts Senior Resource Fair in Coldwater; Breaks Ground at MSU's FRIB Site
Weekly Update
Hosted Senior Resource Fair in Coldwater to connect seniors with resources from the community. Most attendees were worried about how the Presidents health care law will take money from Medicare
Held five successful Coffee Hours in Springport, Brooklyn, Eaton Rapids, Charlotte, and Whitmore Lake
Spoke and participated at Michigan State Universitys groundbreaking for the Facility for Rare Isotope Beams. The facility will allow scientists to better understand nuclear physics.
Met with Michigan Department of Natural Resources officials and staff from the Eisenhower Center to discuss ways in which we can make the Sharonville State Game Area in Washtenaw and Jackson County more accessible and enjoyable for our returning wounded warriors
Met with home Monroe and Lenawee Home Builders Association members and discussed the federal government's excessive regulations and it's negative effect on job creation
