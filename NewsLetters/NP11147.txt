Dear Friend,
It's official! Last night the President signed into law legislation
that I introduced, the Philippines Charitable Giving Assistance Act.
Monetary donations for Typhoon Haiyan relief made from now through
April 14, 2014 can be deducted on your 2013 taxes. Returns for 2013
taxes must be filed by April 15, 2014.
If you have any questions about H.R. 3771, please contact me. You can
do so through my website, swalwell.house.gov. In addition, on
swalwell.house.gov you can view my voting record. You also can connect
with me on Twitter and Facebook.
Sincerely,
