Wisdom of the District: The Vietnam Veterans Among Us
Did you know that nearly 9,000,000 Americans served during the Vietnam Era?
This year the Department of Defense is commemorating the 50th anniversary of the Vietnam War to thank and honor veterans of the Vietnam War, including personnel who were held as prisoners of war or listed as missing in action.
Our office is working on a summer event to honor the Vietnam Veterans in VA-02 and remember those who died in service to our country.
