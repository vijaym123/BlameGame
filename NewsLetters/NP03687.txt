Happy New
Year
The New Year is upon us, and with that a brand new Congress.
As we kick off 2015 with new resolutions and new opportunities, were
excited to get to back to work. From the McKinley family and staff,
we wish you a year full of joy, peace, and happiness.
Happy New Year!
The Year in
Review: 2014 By the Numbers
With 2014 over and
the new Congress about to begin, we took a look at our work over the
past year.
Heres a look at 2014, by the
numbers:
Cast
563 roll call votes in the House
and
missed no votes.
Introduced 14 bills,
bringing the total number of bills introduced to 53 in the 113 th
Congress.
Successfully offered 13
amendments on the House floor or in committee.
Participated
in
38 hearings and legislative markups
with the
Energy and Commerce Committee.
Held just under 250
meetings in the district
in all 20
counties.
Organized 78 roundtable meetings in the First District
with veterans, seniors, small businesses, manufacturers, labor,
teachers, farmers, elected officials, and others.
Conducted
4 telephone town halls
reaching over 40,000
constituents.
Opened
856 constituent casework
cases and
closed 862 cases .
Sent over
34,000 emails and letters
to
constituents.
We look forward to making 2015 and the 114 th
Congress even more successful.
