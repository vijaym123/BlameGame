August 11: The National Debt Workshop
What:
The Concord Coalition, a non-partisan budget policy group, facilitates an interactive exercise, Making the Difficult Policy Choices to Improve Americas Long-Term Fiscal Outlook, that allows participants to learn about balancing the federal budget.
When:
Monday August 11, 6:00pm - 8:00pm
Who:
Open to the public
Where:
Rufty-Holmes Senior Center
1120 S Martin Luther King, Jr. Ave
Salisbury, NC 28144

August 22: Veterans Information Session
What:
Veterans can learn more about available healthcare, education, and financial options with representatives from the VA, the North Carolina Division of Veterans Affairs and veterans service non-profit organizations. Representatives of these groups will be on site to discuss benefits available to those who have served in our armed forces.
When:
Friday, August 22, 3:00pm - 6:00 pm
Who:
All veterans and their families are invited to attend
Where:
Broyhill Conference Center
3450 Clemmons Road
Clemmons, NC 27012
No RSVP necessary
