Traveling Michigan's 7th District
From Monroe to Adrian to Lansing, this week I traveled across Michigan's 7th Congressional District meeting with educators, small business owners, and local leaders. We discussed ways to grow the economy from the ground up to help get people back to work, make life more affordable, and increase opportunity for everyone.
