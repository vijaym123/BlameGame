Celebrating Our Nation's Independence
It has been a pleasure to celebrate our nations independence with the people of the 24th District. On this holiday, let us remember the remarkable courage of our Founding Fathers in declaring our nation's independence, and the incredible struggle to secure and preserve the freedom we all cherish.
