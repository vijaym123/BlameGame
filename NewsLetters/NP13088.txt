Weekly Update
Voted in favor or H.R 3362, the
Exchange Information Disclosure Act , requiring the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) provide weekly status reports on the troubled healthcare law

Voted to keep the government open for the next fiscal year while also cutting total discretionary spending for the fourth consecutive year
Heard testimony from the Chief Information Security Officers at the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services and HHS about the lack of security testing of HealthCare.gov prior to its launch on October 1, 2013
Attended Oversight &amp; Government Reform Committee hearing focusing on the role Inspectors General play in reducing waste, fraud, abuse, and mismanagement, while improving efficiency within federal agencies
Spoke on the House floor about the sanctity of life and to recognize the 40 th
annual March for Life next week
