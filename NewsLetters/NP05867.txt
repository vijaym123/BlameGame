Rep. Miller Receives Award from Manufacturers Association for Outstanding Support of Manufacturing Industry in
Congress
Rep. Candice Miller scored 97% on key manufacturing votes during the 113 th
Congress
Harrison Township, MI
U.S. Representative Candice Miller (MI-10) was awarded the National Association of Manufacturers (NAM) Award for Manufacturing Legislative Excellence for her voting record during the 113 th
Congress
in support of the U.S. manufacturing industry.
Here in Southeast Michigan, manufacturing is what we do best because of the people who live here and work here , Miller said.
Its the work ethic
and commitment of those who work here that make this region the heartland of American innovation. And those of us who serve this region in Congress have a responsibility to pursue policies that support American manufacturing and its workers, and that is a
responsibility that I take very seriously.
Tomorrow,
October 3 rd
at 8:30 a.m.,
in celebration of
National Manufacturing Day, Rep. Miller will join Macomb County Executive Mark Hackel, Macomb Intermediate School District Superintendent Michael DeVault, and students from Utica High School to
tour KUKA Systems North America, LLC., located in Sterling Heights, Michigan.
Members of Congress that received the Award for Manufacturing Legislative Excellence consistently supported the manufacturing community during the 113th Congress (20132014) and maintained a voting record of 70
percent or higher in support of the industry.
For the 113 th
Congress, Rep. Candice Miller scored 97%.
For more information, please visit the National Association of Manufacturers online:
http://www.nam.org/
