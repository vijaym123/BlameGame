Maine
Farmland Trust to Receive Federal Grant : U.S. Senators Susan
Collins and Angus King and Congressman Bruce Poliquin announced that the
Maine Farmland Trust in Belfast will receive $249,816 from the U.S.
Department of Agriculture (USDA). Healthy food helps to make healthy
families, and its important that Maines most vulnerable citizens have
access to fresh fruits and vegetables, said Senators Collins, and
King, and Congressman Poliquin in a joint statement .
In Maine, we are lucky enough to have a vibrant local food movement,
and this investment will make locally-grown produce more accessible for
people with limited means, while supporting our farmers in the
process.
