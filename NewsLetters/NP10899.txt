April 7, 2014
Dear Fellow Nevadan,
I am
pleased to announce that the
Small Business
Administration
(SBA) will present a webinar series to help small
disadvantaged businesses learn more about the SBAs 8(a) Business Development
program. On April 9 th , the SBA will present How to Prepare and
Submit Your 8(a) Business Development Application-Tips for Success,
the first in a series of webinars beginning in April and ending in
August.
During the first webinar, small business owners will
learn the following:
How the 8(a) Program works
Eligibility requirements for 8(a) certification
Benefits of
the 8(a) Program
Helpful tips on completing an 8(a)
application
The webinar series has been specifically designed to
help small disadvantaged businesses obtain their 8(a) certification,
which helps make them more competitive in the federal marketplace.
Pre-registration is not required for this event. Simply click on this
link
to participate on Wednesday, April
9 th
from 11 a.m.
12 p.m. PST.
I hope you find this
information helpful. For more information on my work on behalf of small
business owners like you, please visit my website,
reid.senate.gov , sign up for my e-newsletter,
The
Reid Report , or connect with me on
Facebook
and
Twitter .
Sincerely,
HARRY REID
