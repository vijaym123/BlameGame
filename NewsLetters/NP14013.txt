November 27, 2013
Dear Friend,
I will be hosting a Telephone Town Hall about the Affordable Care Act,
also known as Obamacare, on Monday, December 2 at 6:35pm CT. Please fill
out the form here
with your home phone
number if you would like to participate and ask questions about how
health care reform can help you.
If you do not have health insurance and would like to sign up, please
visit www.healthcare.gov
to shop for plans and sign up.
As always, I remain,
Most sincerely,
