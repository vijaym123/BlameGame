Congressman Lamborn
Cosponsors Veterans Suicide Prevention
Bill
10/6/14
Today, Congressman Doug Lamborn cosponsored the bipartisan H.R. 5059,
the Clay Hunt Suicide Prevention for American Veterans Act. Clay was a
Marine veteran of Afghanistan and Iraq, a Purple Heart recipient, and
an advocate for veterans. Sadly, in 2011, Clay took his own
life.
Major elements of the legislation
include:
-Requires a 3rd party to annually evaluate DoD and VA mental
health/suicide prevention programs.
-Requires the VA to
develop an interactive website to raise awareness of mental health
services.
-Includes provisions to ensure Guard and Reserve is
not left out.
-Addresses the lack of mental health
professionals through an education loan repayment pilot program for
individuals pursuing psychiatric medicine.
-Leverages community
assets through a piloted community reintegration
program.
