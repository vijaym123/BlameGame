Veteran Receives Long-Overdue Silver Star
Earlier this
month, Congressman Van Hollen and Brigadier General Jeffrey Clark,
Commander of the Walter Reed National Military Medical Center,
presented George C. Bloodworth the Silver Star Medal that he had earned
in Vietnam, but had never actually received.
Read more >>
Van Hollen Celebrates Lunar New Year
Congressman Van Hollen wishes the Asian American and
Pacific Islander community a happy, healthy and prosperous Lunar New
Year.
Watch the video >>
Fighting to Extend Emergency Unemployment Insurance
Congressman Van Hollen testified before
the House Rules Committee this week in an effort to fight for a vote to
extend emergency unemployment insurance for 1.6 million struggling
Americans and their families using savings from excessive agriculture
subsidies.
Watch the video >>
Affordable Care Act: Key Date to Know
Individuals and families in Maryland have until March 31, 2014 to
enroll in a health insurance plan and possibly qualify for financial
assistance through Maryland Health Connection. Enrollment in Medicaid
is year-round.
If you experience any difficulties enrolling in coverage, please feel
free to call my District office for assistance at (301) 424-3501.
