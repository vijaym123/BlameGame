Friends,
I would like to invite you, as a resident of the Third District of
Michigan, to town halls
I am hosting in Battle Creek and Grand Rapids on Wednesday, January 22,
and Thursday, January 23. I will discuss matters under consideration in
Congress, and I look forward to hearing from you and answering your
questions.
The Battle Creek town hall will be held from 5:30 p.m. to 6:30 p.m. on
Wednesday, January 22, at W. K. Kellogg Gymnasium at 60 West Van Buren
Street
.
The Grand Rapids town hall will be held from 5:30 p.m. to 6:30 p.m. on
Thursday, January 23, at University Prep Academy at 512 South Division
Avenue
.
These town halls are free and open to the public. Please contact my
Grand Rapids office at (616) 451-8383 or my Battle Creek office at
(269) 205-3823 if you have any further questions.
Sincerely,
Justin Amash
Member of Congress
