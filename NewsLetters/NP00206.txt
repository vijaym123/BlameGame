Sharing a Powerful Story on Veteran
Suicide
6/10/15
Today, in the House Veterans Affairs
Oversight and Investigations Subcommittee, Congressman Doug Lamborn
participated in a hearing on Prescription Mismanagement and the Risk of
Veteran Suicide.
The
Congressman shared some powerful and emotional testimony on a brave local
soldier our community recently lost. He encourages everyone to take a few
minutes to watch this video and lift the family up in your thoughts
and prayers.
