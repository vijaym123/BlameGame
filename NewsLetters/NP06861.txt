Dear Fellow Nevadan,
It is with pleasure
that I invite you to the
Faith Forum 2014
on Thursday, August 28, 2014, at Nevada Partners. This interactive
forum will give you the opportunity to learn about federal resources,
programs, and policy that can help your faith organization better
achieve its mission as we work together to enrich our
community.
This event will benefit your congregations leadership staff and
members who do community outreach. Attendees will have the opportunity to
receive the latest updates from local and national agencies on best
practices, grant opportunities, and community resources. Lunch will be
provided.
RSVP today at
rsvp@reid.senate.gov
or call my Las Vegas office at (702) 388-5020.
