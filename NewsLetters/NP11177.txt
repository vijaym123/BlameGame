Dear Friend,
I'm writing to invite you to Women's Business Empowerment Day on
Saturday, April 12 at CSU East Bay in Hayward. This event was inspired
by the principle that when women succeed, America succeeds. This is the
central tenet of my Women's Economic Agenda, which aims to address the
economic challenges facing women and families today.
Women's Business Empowerment Day
Saturday, April 12, 2014
9:30 AM - 12:00 PM (doors open at 8:30 AM for registration and
networking)
CSU East Bay, Valley Business and Technology Center
Carlos Bee Boulevard, Hayward, CA 94542 (MAP
)
Parking and attendance are free. Park in lots G & H.
Our event is an effort to provide women of the East Bay with the tools
necessary to succeed in the workforce. The day will feature workshops
on a number of skills including:
*	How to start or expand your own small business
*	Perfecting the art of negotiation: salary, benefits, and
work/family balance
*	Achieving leadership within your company and community
We have invited a distinguished panel of speakers and hope you will
join us for a collaborative and informative event. To RSVP or learn
more or please visit: http://womensbizday.eventbrite.com
or contact my office. A full list of confirmed speakers
can be found on the event page.
It is an honor to serve as your representative in Congress. Please
contact my East Bay offices at 510-370-3322 or 925-460-5100 if you have
any further questions or if we can be of service in any way. To stay
connected, please follow me on Twitter and join me on Facebook.
Sincerely,
Eric Swalwell
