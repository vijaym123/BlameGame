House Passes Walberg Veterans Bill, Votes to Reverse President's Immigration Actions
Weekly Update
Joined my House colleagues in voting to reverse the Presidents executive actions on immigration and clarify that only Congress can write our immigration laws.
The House passed my proposal to increase resources for new veterans as they transition to civilian life as part of the National Defense Authorization Act.
Voted for legislation to prevent tax hikes on small businesses, manufacturers, and families in Michigan.
Helped pass the ABLE Act, a bill I cosponsored to help empower individuals with disabilities.
Met with the Monroe County Substance Abuse Coalition and presented their Drug-Free Communities grant award.
