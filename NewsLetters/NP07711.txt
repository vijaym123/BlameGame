Weekly Update
House passed my bipartisan bill, H.R. 3534, to rename the U.S. Post Office located at 113 West Michigan Ave in Jackson after police officer James Bonneau, who died in the line of duty in 2010
Voted to provide nearly $11 billion for highway funding and continue federal highway and transportation infrastructure projects through May 2015
Voted for H.R. 5016, the Financial Services and General Government Appropriations Act, to limit IRS overreach and abuse and which included an amendment I offered to prohibit IRS employees from violating the Federal Records Act
Attended Education and the Workforce Subcommittee hearing to examine efforts to assist missing and exploited children
Attended the Silver Maples of Chelsea Evening of Civil Discourse and gave an update on my work to create jobs, promote education, and rein in federal debt
