Weekly Update
Spoke on the House floor about the high cost of energy and how an all-of-the-above strategy will lower costs and help grow American jobs
Helped pass H.R. 4810, the Veterans Access to Care Act, legislation I cosponsored to help alleviate the extraordinarily long wait times at the VA healthcare system
Voted in favor of H.R. 4475, Americas Small Business Tax Relief Act, to provide certainty to small business owners by making expensing levels outlined in section 179 of the tax code permanent
Attended Oversight &amp; Government Reform hearing examining the integrity of Social Security Administrations disability determination appeals process
Briefed on the Presidents strategy and engagement in Afghanistan post-2014 and on the situation surrounding the Sgt. Bowe Bergdahl transfer
Spoke with students from Potterville Middle School on the U.S. Capitol steps about recently passed legislation and answered questions about how Congress works
