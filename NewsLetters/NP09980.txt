Rep. Massie Talks IRS and Benghazi with Gretchen Carlson of Fox News
Congressman Massie, a member of the House Oversight &amp; Government Reform Committee, talked about yesterday's House vote to hold ex-IRS official Lois Lerner in contempt of Congress. He also shared his thoughts on the formation of a Select Committee to investigate Benghazi.

Rep. Massie talks about Raw Milk and Food &amp; Farm Freedom on Fox Business
Congressman Massie recently introduced two Milk Freedom bills. He talked with the hosts of The Independents about the momentum his bipartisan bills are gaining.

Rep. Massie Investigates ATF Raid on Ares Firearms
Recently, the ATF raided Ares Armor in California and seized the names of their customers. Ares Armor contends publicly they are not a gun manufacturer. Congressman Massie suggested that the ATF should relinquish the customer list if Ares Armor is found not guilty in the criminal investigation.
