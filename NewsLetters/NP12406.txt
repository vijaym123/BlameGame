CONTACT ME
February 2014
Dear Neighbors,
Please join me on Thursday, February 20th for a Hiring Event I am
hosting in conjunction with Congresswoman Tammy Duckworth. I know how
valuable meeting face-to-face with potential employers is, and I am
confident that this Hiring Event will offer many exciting opportunities
to make connections, learn valuable strategies and potentially find
your next job.
You can pre-register and find more information on this Hiring Event
through this form
on my website. If you have any
questions or need any assistance, please contact my District Office at
(847) 793-0625.
I hope to see you on the 20th!
Thank you,
Brad Schneider

317 Cannon HOB
Washington, DC 20515
Phone: (202) 225-4835	Lincolnshire Office
