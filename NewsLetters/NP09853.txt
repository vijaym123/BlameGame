SHARE ON:
Rep Walberg Supports Tax Relief for Job Creators;
Expanding Access to Quality Charter Schools
Weekly Update
Voted in favor of H.R. 4438,
the American Research and Competitiveness Act , to provide businesses with a permanent research and development tax credit
Voted for H.R. 10, the
Success and Opportunity through Quality Charter Schools Act ,
bipartisan legislation to promote state efforts to develop and expand charter schools while supporting choice, innovation and excellence in education
Supported the creation of a Select Committee on the Benghazi attacks and voted to hold Lois Lerner in contempt of Congress for her refusal to answer questions about the targeting of conservative groups by the IRS
Examined the consequences of negative effects of a National Labor Relations Board decision allowing the unionization of student athletes during an Education and Workforce Committee hearing
Held Coffee Hours in Bronson and Jonesville where I gave an update and heard constituent concerns about out-of-control government spending
Toured Nyloncraft, a major manufacturer in Hillsdale County that has since expanded and added jobs
