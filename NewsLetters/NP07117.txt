Dear

Welcome to my
Congress in your Inbox

edition you'll find information about the recent House compromise to
address Veterans health care , as well as my letter to
House GOP Leadership urging them to reduce the
nation's

Shero of the Week , highlighting an interview I did about my role in

Twitter.

edition of
Congress in your Inbox.
