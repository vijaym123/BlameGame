Dear Friend,
There were many noteworthy moments in 2013, from a new district office
in San Tan Valley to the tragedy in Yarnell. Below are 13 pictures of
the most memorable moments. I look forward to many more next year.
(My daughter Elly and I before going to the White House Holiday Ball,
December 10, 2013)
(Surveying lettuce fields in Yuma, December 7, 2013)
(Speaking at a rally against the Obama Administration's war on coal,
October 29, 2013)
(Meeting with members of the Yavapai County Republicans, October 24,
2013)
(Attempting to ride a skateboard at the dedication of the Tinnell
Memorial Sports Park in Lake Havasu City, October 19, 2013)
(On the first anniversary of the terrorist attacks in Benghazi, I
hand-delivered a letter to the State Department asking for answers
about the attacks, September 11, 2013)
(Speaking alongside Governor Brewer and other members of Arizona's
Congressional Delegation about the Obama Administration's denial of
disaster relief to the Yarnell area after the Yarnell Hill Fire,
September 4, 2013)
(Cutting the ribbon for a new mining exhibit at the Community Center in
Bullhead City, August 20, 2013)
(I met with a group of Teen Age Republicans from Arizona in the Rayburn
Room of the US Capitol, July 18, 2013)
(Observing the Yarnell Hill Fire before attending a memorial service
for the 19 fallen Granite Mountain Hotshots in Prescott, July 1, 2013)
(Demanding a full repeal of ObamaCare during a Congressional GOP
Doctors Caucus press conference, May 16, 2013)
(Local volunteers and I cleaned part of Highway 89 in Chino Valley,
April 6, 2013)
(Talking with constituents at the opening of my district office in San
Tan Valley, February 21, 2013)
As always, you can follow everything I am working on in Arizona and
Washington, D.C. through my website (http://gosar.house.gov
) on Twitter @repgosar
, or through Facebook
at Representative Paul Gosar.
Sincerely,
Paul A. Gosar, D.D.S.
Member of Congress
