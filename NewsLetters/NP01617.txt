Easter 2015 Statement
4/3/15
To commemorate Easter,
Congressman Doug Lamborn has issued the following
statement:
"For thousands of years, the
miracle of the resurrection has brought hope, comfort, and joy to millions
of people all over the world. It is profoundly humbling to think
of the grace and mercy shown to us because of this great
day. Please join me in wishing everyone a restful, fulfilling, and very Happy
Easter."
- Congressman
Doug Lamborn (CO-05)
