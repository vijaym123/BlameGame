Last week, a Malaysia Airlines passenger flight, carrying nearly 300 people, was shot down over Ukraine.
Secretary of State John Kerry stated that there is
evidence
that Russia provided the rebels in Ukraine with the weapons used to shoot down the plane, and
reports
indicate that the rebels have turned over the black boxes that contain key data about the flight and the downing of the plane.
This week, the United Nations Security Council voted unanimously to conduct an international investigation into the tragic incident.
