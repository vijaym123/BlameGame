Please call Congressman Denny Heck's District Office at (360) 459-8514

How Can We Help You?
As your representative, Congressman Heck works to ensure you receive
necessary assistance and resources. He is committed to making
government more accessible, transparent and responsive.
Congress At Your Corner
Congress At Your Corner is an opportunity for you to meet one on one
with the person who represents your interests in Congress. Please join
Congressman Heck in Lacey or Parkland later this month to address any
questions or concerns.
Wednesday, May 14, 5:30 - 8:30 p.m.
Lacey City Hall, Council Chamber & Executive Board Room
420 College St., S.E., Lacey, WA, 98503
Tuesday, May 27, 5:30 - 8:30 p.m.
Pierce County Library Administration Building
3005 112th St., S.E., Parkland, WA, 98446
Federal Agency Assistance
Constituent Services Representatives are available to help you navigate
federal agencies and, when necessary, direct you to the appropriate
state or local agency to answer your questions and respond to your
requests. Please see below dates and times that representatives will be
in your area.

Lakewood Community Center, Senior Activity Center

Library North Meeting Room, Puyallup Public Library
