Rep. Renacci Sends Letter to Secretary Kathleen
Sebelius
Earlier this week, I sent a letter to Health and Human Services
Secretary (HHS) Kathleen Sebelius requesting information regarding
problems with the 834 EDI transmission form, a back-end reporting tool
that is central to health insurers and the successful enrollment of
many Ohioans and Americans across the country who are signing up for
health care plans through the exchanges. Read more HERE
.

Weekly Column: Working Toward A Real Economic Recovery
Last week, the United States Department of Labor released its latest
unemployment report, which indicated that the U.S. economy added
203,000 jobs this past month. While this is encouraging, we must not
lose sight of the fact that the labor force participation rate remains
the lowest it has been in decades. With that in mind, our economic
outlook is far more troubling than the latest jobs report suggests.
Read more HERE
.
_____
House Ways and Means Committee Approves The Medicare
Patient Access And Quality Improvement Act
This week, the House Ways & Means Committee voted to approve H.R. 2810,
the Medicare Patient Access and Quality Improvement Act. This
legislation repeals the flawed Sustainable Growth Rate (SGR) formula
and replaces traditional fee-for-service with a payment model that
rewards physicians for quality, rather than quantity. Read more HERE
.
