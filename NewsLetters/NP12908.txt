Dear Friend.
This evening President Obama will deliver his annual State of the Union
Address. Please join me for a Telephone Town Hall on Thursday, January
30 at 7:00 p.m. Pacific Time. You'll have the chance to discuss the
State of the Union, hear my priorities for 2014, and share your
priorities with me.

using a land line.

.
