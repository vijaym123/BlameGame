Recap of Ebola Public Information
Session
10/30/14
Last night, Congressman Doug Lamborn brought
together together key leaders within our local civilian and military
medical communities to host a public information session on Ebola
response in the Pikes Peak region.
"After listening to these presentations, I think you'll agree with
me that trained and dedicated professionals are working hard and
planning intelligently to protect our community."
- Congressman Doug
Lamborn (CO-05)
