VETERANS RESOURCE FAIR
I am holding a Veterans Resource Fair in Lancaster on Monday, July 7, 2014.
All veterans are invited to attend the event.
There will be experts on hand to answer questions and provide assistance for veterans regarding their benefits including issues such as: disability compensation, health care, employment support, GI Bill and the Ohio Veterans Bonus. For further information or questions on the resource fair, please contact Adam Rapien in my office at (614) 771-4968.
The Veterans Resource Fair will take place:
ON: Monday, July 7, 2014
FROM:
9:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m.
AT: The Liberty Center
951 Liberty Dr.
Lancaster, OH, 43130
The following organizations will be on hand to participate in the Veterans Resource Fair:
Fairfield County Veterans Service Commission, Pickaway County Veterans Service Commission, Hocking County Veterans Service Commission, Cleveland VA Regional Office, Chalmers P. Wylie VA Ambulatory Care Center, Chillicothe VA Medical Center, Ohio Department of Veterans Services, Hero 2 Hired, American Legion, AMVETS, Veterans of Foreign Wars, Disabled American Veterans, Military Order of the Purple Heart, Ohio National Guard Family Readiness, and the Warrior Support Program.
