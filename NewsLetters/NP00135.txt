As part of a larger effort to improve government transparency and keep the constituents of Pennsylvania's 4th Congressional District informed of current legislative business, I send out updates at the beginning of each Session week as an overview of legislative matters to be addressed. Please click on my photo below to listen to the audio clip.
As always, if you have questions or concerns on any issue or need help with a federal agency, please contact our offices anytime
