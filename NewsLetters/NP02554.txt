Student Art Competition Deadline: March 2
The deadline to enter Rep. Becerra's 2015 Congressional Student Art Competition is Monday, March 2.
Watch a video of the 2014 Student Art Competition Winner Benjamin Hunter
The first place winner of the competition receives an all expense paid trip to Washington D.C. to attend a national ceremony and has his/her artwork displayed in the U.S. Capitol for one year.
All high school students who live in
California's 34th Congressional District
are eligible to participate in this year's competition.
To learn more about the student art competition, including important rules and guidelines, visit Rep. Becerra's
Student Art Competition web page.
