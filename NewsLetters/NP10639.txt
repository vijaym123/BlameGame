Rep. Miller Announces Winners of This Years Congressional Art Competition
Harrison Township, MI
Today, U.S. Representative Candice Miller (MI-10) announced the winners of this years 2014 Artistic Discovery Art Competition.
This year, 116 pieces of artwork from Michigans 10th Congressional District were
entered into the contest.
Congratulations to this years competition winners. It was a tough choice for our judges, with 116 amazing pieces of artwork to review,
Miller said after announcing the 2014 winners.
This great exhibit is a testament to the true artistic talent of our young local artist.
They should all be very proud.
