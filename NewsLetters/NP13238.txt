
JOIN ME ON
January 1, 2014
The New Year is always a hopeful time, as we celebrate the end of
one year and the beginning of another. Please accept my sincere wishes
for a happy, healthy, and safe New Year. On New Year's, we look with
hope to the year ahead and the opportunities it will bring. On behalf
of Christy and our daughters, I wish you a safe, healthy, and happy New
Year. May God bless you and your family, and may God bless America.
