
News

June 10, 2014
As part of a larger effort to improve government transparency and keep the constituents of Pennsylvania's 4th Congressional District informed of current legislative business, I send out weekly updates at the beginning of each week that Congress is in session. These updates are a brief overview of upcoming legislative matters to be addressed each week. Please click on my photo below to listen to the provided audio clip.
As always, if you have any other questions or concerns on any issue or need help with a federal agency, please contact one of my offices anytime.
CLICK ON PICTURE TO PLAY SHORT AUDIO
H.R. 2072
- Demanding Accountability for Veterans Act - Would improve the accountability of the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to the Inspector General of the Department of Veterans Affairs (Sponsored by Rep. Dan Benishek/Veterans Affairs Committee)
H.Res. 600
- Would urge the Government of Afghanistan, following a successful first round of the presidential election on April 5, 2014, to pursue a transparent, credible, and inclusive run-off presidential election on June 14, 2014, while ensuring the safety of voters, candidates, poll workers, and election observers (Sponsored by Rep. Alan Grayson/Foreign Affairs Committee)
H.R. 4412
- The National Aeronautics and Space Administration Authorization Act of 2014 - Would authorize the programs of the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (Sponsored by Rep. Steven Palazzo/Science, Space, and Technology Committee)
S. 1254
- Harmful Algal Bloom and Hypoxia Research and Control Amendments Act of 2013 - Would amend the Harmful Algal Blooms and Hypoxia Research and Control Act of 1998 (Sponsored by Sen. Bill Nelson/Science, Space, and Technology Committee)
H.Con.Res. 100
- Would authorize the use of the rotunda of the Capitol for a ceremony to commemorate the 50th anniversary of the enactment of the Civil Rights Act (Sponsored by Rep. Marcia Fudge/House Administration Committee)
S.Con.Res. 36
- Would permit the use of the rotunda of the Capitol for a ceremony to award the Congressional Gold Medal to the next of kin or personal representative of Raoul Wallenberg (Sponsored by Sen. Kirsten Gillibrand/House Administration Committee)
H.R. 3211
- Mortgage Choice Act - Would amend the Truth in Lending Act to improve upon the definitions provided for points and fees in connection with a mortgage transaction (Sponsored by Rep. Bill Huizenga/Financial Services Committee)
H.R. 1679
- Would amend the Expedited Funds Availability Act to clarify the application of that Act to American Samoa (Sponsored by Rep. Eni Faleomavaega/Financial Services Committee)
H.R. 4228
- DHS Acquisition Accountability and Efficiency Act - Would require the Department of Homeland Security to improve discipline, accountability, and transparency in acquisition program management (Sponsored by Rep. Jeff Duncan/Homeland Security Committee)
H.R. 4745
- Transportation, Housing, and Urban Development, and Related Agencies Appropriations Act, 2015 - Would set appropriations for the Departments of Transportation, and Housing and Urban Development, and related agencies for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2015 (Sponsored by Rep. Tom Latham/Appropriations Committee)
H.R. 4810
- The Veterans Access to Care Act - Would direct the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to enter into contracts for the provision of hospital care and medical services at non-Department of Veterans Affairs facilities for Department of Veterans Affairs patients with extended waiting times for appointments at Department facilities (Sponsored by Rep. Jeff Miller/Veterans Affairs Committee)
H.Res. 608
- Would condemn the senseless rampage and mass shooting that took place in Isla Vista, California, on Friday May 23, 2014 (Sponsored by Rep. Lois Capps/Oversight and Government Reform Committee)
H.R. 4880
- Agriculture, Rural Development, Food and Drug Administration, and Related Agencies Appropriations Act, 2015 - Would set appropriations for the Agriculture, Rural Development, Food and Drug Administration, and related agencies programs for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2015 (Sponsored by Rep. Robert Aderholt/Appropriations Committee)
H.R. 4453
- Permanent S Corporation Built-in Gains Recognition Period Act of 2014 - Would make permanent the reduced recognition period for built-in gains of S corporations (Sponsored by Rep. David Reichert/Ways and Means Committee)
H.R. 4457
- Americas Small Business Tax Relief Act of 2014 - Would permanently extend increased expensing limitations (Sponsored by Rep. Pat Tiberi/Ways and Means Committee)
