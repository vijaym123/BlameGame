Nurse Appreciation Event
This past week, I visited UH Ahuja Medical Center to speak with nurses
about their concerns regarding policy set forth in Washington. As the
son of a nurse, I know just how committed these professionals are. I
truly appreciate their hard work and their dedication to their patients.
_____
Need Help With A Federal Agency?
If you can't get an answer from a federal agency in a timely fashion,
or if you feel you have been treated unfairly, my office may be able to
help resolve a problem or get you the information that you need. While
we cannot guarantee you a favorable outcome, we will do our best to
help you receive a fair and timely response. My office holds Mobile
Office Hours throughout Ohio's 16thDistrict as a way to assist you at a
location that is convenient for you. Visit our website to learn more
about our Mobile Office Hours and how we can help you navigate federal
agencies
.
_____
We Need To Make America Stronger
As I travel throughout Ohio's 16th District I consistently hear the
same resounding message: we need to make America stronger. From
Strongsville to Wooster, Northeast Ohioans want a healthier economy.
Americans across the country want prosperity restored and an America
where hard work opens the door to success and infinite opportunity.
