Congressional Report
Dear Friends,
Please take a moment to read my latest update from this week in Washington and Texas Congressional District 28. You can stay connected by signing up for my weekly email
here.
Discussing the Importance of the U.S.-Mexico Border Economy
I met with a panel, including fellow Rep. Beto O'Rourke (D-TX16), on Feb. 3 at the Wilson Center in Washington to talk about the impact and importance of the U.S.-Mexico economy on the border.
Meeting with McAllen Delegation About Border Infrastructure
Members from the McAllen Delegation and I met recently in D.C. to discuss border infrastructure at our bridges and ports of entry. Pictured from left to right are McAllen Mayor Jim Darling, Julian Alvarez with Rio Grande Partnership, myself, Sam Vale with Starr-Camargo Bridge and Jesse Hereford with Border Trade Alliance.
Working with Gov. Abbot's Office for TX28
On Feb. 3, my Chief of Staff Cynthia Gaona met with Jerry Strickland, Executive Director of the Office of State and Federal Relations for new Texas Governor Greg Abbot to discuss the shared goals of new Texas Governor Greg Abbot and District 28.
Meeting with SBA and HHS to discuss the Affordable Care Act
The Small Business Administration (SBA) and the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) visited with my San Antonio District Office last week to discuss issues regarding the Affordable Care Act. These federal agencies have worked with my office and community organizations to provide information on the ACA and health care enrollment.
ACA Workshop in Rio Grande City
My office hosted an Affordable Health Care workshop on Jan. 31 at the Starr County Courthouse Annex in Rio Grande City. Several families made the decision to get health coverage via
HealthCare.gov . The deadline to apply for coverage is Feb. 15.
