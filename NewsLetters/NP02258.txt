Visiting Cogitic
Corporation
3/12/15
Today, Congressman Doug Lamborn visited co-owners Jonathan and Jared
Veteto and the entire team at Cogitic Corporation. Headquartered right
here in Colorado Springs,
Cogitic
makes high quality parts for America's submarine fleet. They were also
involved in the commissioning of the USS Colorado, a brand new
Virginia-class submarine that had her keel laying ceremony on March
7th.
