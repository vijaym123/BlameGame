Announcing Military Academy
Information Day 2014
Dear Friend,
On Sunday, May 25, I will be hosting an information session for area
students and families on our nation's military academies, including the
U.S. Military Academy at West Point, the U.S. Naval Academy, the U.S.
Merchant Marine Academy, the U.S. Air Force Academy, and the U.S. Coast
Guard Academy.
The event will take place at Jenkintown High School and will offer area
students and families the opportunity to meet directly with
representatives from the service academies and get the answers to
frequently asked questions such as, "What's it like to attend a
military academy?" "How do I apply?" and "What qualifications do I need
to have to be considered?"
Staff from my district office will also be available to answer
questions concerning the Congressional nomination process to military
academies. This event is free and open to the general public.
WHO:
Staff from Rep. Allyson Schwartz's district office and representatives
from the U.S. Military Academy at West Point, the U.S. Naval Academy,
the U.S. Merchant Marine Academy, the U.S. Air Force Academy, and U.S.
Coast Guard Academy.
WHEN:
Sunday, May 25, 2014, 10:00 a.m. - 12:00 p.m.
WHERE:
Jenkintown High School, 325 Highland Avenue Jenkintown, PA 19046
For more information, please contact:
Douglas Webb (215) 517-6572 / douglas.webb@mail.house.gov
Sincerely,
Allyson Y. Schwartz
Member of Congress
