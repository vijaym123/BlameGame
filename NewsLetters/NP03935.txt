Congressman Lamborn Attends Veterans Affairs Subcommittee Discussing
National Cemeteries
12/9/14
Today, at 11:00am
MT/ 1:00pm ET, Congressman Doug Lamborn will be participating in a
hearing of the House Veterans Affairs Subcommittee on Disability Assistance
and Memorial Affairs. The hearing is entitled, Timeless Honor:
Reviewing Current Operations of our National Cemeteries." The
Congressman will be questioning the panel on the progress of the forthcoming
Pikes Peak National VA Cemetery.
Please click here to watch this hearing:
http://www.ustream.tv/channel/hclive14
