Honoring Korean War
Veterans
7/28/14
This past weekend, Congressman Doug Lamborn participated in an
event honoring Korean War veterans at Memorial Park in Colorado
Springs. The Congressman presented a Korean Peace Ambassador medal to
veteran Russ Ericson. Also present were Korean Cons ul General Dong-Man
Han,
the President of the USAFA Endowment,
retired Air
Force General Stephen Lorenz, County Commissioner Sallie Clark, City
Councilor Helen Collins, and the Widefield High School JROTC served as the
Color Guard.
"Thank you to the veterans who defended the Korean
peninsula from being swallowed whole by communism. The efforts of these
brave heroes should never be forgotten."
- Congressman Doug
Lamborn
Widefield High School Color Guard (left to right): Craig Hubbs,
Michael Hatch, Rhys Nelson, Justin Shiner, Kyle Holdcraft, Isaiah
Vargas, Matthew Klausch
