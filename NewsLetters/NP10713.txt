Congressman Lamborn's Subcommittee Discusses Jobs for Women and

4/2/14
TODAY at 12:00pm MT/ 2:00pm ET,
Congressman Doug Lamborn will chair a hearing of his Energy and Mineral
Resources Subcommittee. The main topic will be the "American Energy Jobs:

Economics, Mr. Dale LeFebvre Founder and Chairman 3.5.7.11 Holding
