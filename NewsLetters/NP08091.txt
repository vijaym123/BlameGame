July 9, 2014
As part of a larger effort to improve government transparency and keep the constituents of Pennsylvania's 4th Congressional District informed of current legislative business, I send out weekly updates at the beginning of each week that Congress is in session. These updates are a brief overview of upcoming legislative matters to be addressed each week. Please click on my photo below to listen to the provided audio clip.
As always, if you have any other questions or concerns on any issue or need help with a federal agency, please contact one of my offices anytime.
CLICK ON PICTURE TO PLAY SHORT AUDIO
H.R. 1528
- Veterinary Medicine Mobility Act - would amend the Controlled Substances Act to allow a veterinarian to transport and dispense controlled substances in the usual course of veterinary practice outside of the registered location (Sponsored by Rep. Kurt Schrader/Energy and Commerce Committee)
H.R. 4653
- would reauthorize the United States Commissions on International Religious Freedom (Sponsored by Rep. Frank Wolf / Foreign Affairs Committee)
H.Res. 588
- concerning the suspension of exit permit issuance by the Government of the Democratic Republic of Congo for adopted Congolese children seeking to depart the country with their adoptive parents (Sponsored by Rep. Collin Peterson/Foreign Affairs Committee)
H.R. 3488
- The Preclearance Authorization Act of 2014 - would establish the conditions under which the Secretary of Homeland Security may establish preclearance facilities, conduct preclearance operations, and provide customs services outside the United States (Sponsored by Rep. Patrick Meehan/Homeland Security Committee)
H.R. 4007
- The Chemical Facility Anti-Terrorism Standards Program Authorization and Accountability Act of 2014 - would recodify and reauthorize the Chemical Facility Anti-Terrorism Standards Program (Sponsored by Rep. Patrick Meehan/Homeland Security Committee)
H.R. 4263
- The Social Media Working Group Act of 2014 - would amend the Homeland Security Act of 2002 to authorize the Department of Homeland Security to establish a social media working group (Sponsored by Rep. Susan Brooks/Homeland Security Committee)
H.R. 4812
- The Honor Flight Act - would require the Administrator of the Transportation Security Administration to establish a process for providing expedited and dignified passenger screening services for veterans traveling to visit war memorials built and dedicated to honor their service (Sponsored by Rep. Cedric Richmond/Homeland Security Committee)
H.R. 4289
- The Department of Homeland Security Interoperable Communications Act - would amend the Homeland Security Act of 2002 to require the Under Secretary for Management of the Department of Homeland Security to take administrative action to achieve and maintain interoperable communications capabilities among the components of the Department of Homeland Security (Sponsored by Rep. Donald Payne/Homeland Security Committee)
H.R. 803
- Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act - would amend the Workforce Investment Act of 1998 to strengthen the United States workforce development system through innovation in, and alignment and improvement of, employment, training, and education programs in the United States, and promote individual and national economic growth (Sponsored by Rep. Virginia Foxx/Education and the Workforce Committee)
H.R. 4923
- Energy and Water Development and Related Agencies Appropriations Act, 2015 - would set appropriations for energy and water development and related agencies for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2015 (Sponsored by Rep. Michael Simpson/Appropriations Committee)
H.R. 4718
- would amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to modify and make permanent bonus depreciation (Sponsored by Rep. Pat Tiberi/Ways and Means Committee)
