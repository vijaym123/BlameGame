March 13, 2015
Thanks to President Obama, millions of
hard-working immigrants who were brought here as children, or who are
parents of U.S. Citizens, will have the opportunity to stay in this
country.
I am proud to join Congressman Luis
Gutirrez, who has launched a national tour to educate immigrants and
allies about what the President announced, how applicants can prepare,
and how everyone else can help make the initiative a success.
Rep. Gutirrez will join me at an event in Los Angeles on Saturday March
21, to help immigrants in the Valley learn about their options and
apply for relief through President Obama's executive action
programs.
We will also have "family defenders" at the
workshop. If you are here without documentation, or you know someone
who is, please RSVP to this event at
RSVP.Cardenas@mail.house.gov , or click "Yes" in the poll to the
right.
