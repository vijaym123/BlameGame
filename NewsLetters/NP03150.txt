House
Republican Israel Caucus Statement On 70th Anniversary of the Liberation
of Auschwitz
U.S.
Representatives Doug Lamborn (CO-05), Leonard Lance (NJ-07), Lee Zeldin (NY-01),
and Peter Roskam (IL-06), co-chairmen of the House Republican Israel
Caucus, released the following statement on the 70th Anniversary of the
Liberation of Auschwitz:
Our Nation will never forget the hate and intolerance, immense
suffering and crimes against humanity perpetuated at Auschwitz.
On
this 70 th
anniversary of its liberation we reflect on the
innocent lives lost during the Holocaust, pay tribute to survivors and
honor the brave work of liberators.
We will never forget the great
sins that claimed the lives of millions and forever scarred the face
of mankind, but on this day we renew our commitment for a world that
works toward peace and justice for all.
