ATTENTION RECREATIONAL BOATERS:
State And Local Authorities To Hold
Recreational Boating Meeting
This message is an announcement of a public meeting
Looking for more information, or have a question, comment, or concern?
Tomorrow morning recreational boaters and fishermen have an opportunity to speak with state and local authorities to prepare for the 2014 recreational boating season. Members of the Virginia Beach Police Department Marine Patrol, U.S. Coast Guard, Virginia Department of Game and Inland Fisheries, the Virginia Marine Resource Commission and the Virginia Beach Fire Department will be on hand to meet with the local boating community to address questions, comments, or concerns.
When: 10 a.m. TOMORROW
Saturday, March 8
Where: First Landing Fire Station No. 1
2837 Shore Drive
Virginia Beach
***NOTE: This town hall meeting is hosted by the agencies listed above.***
