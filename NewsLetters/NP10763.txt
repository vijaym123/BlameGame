Welch demands disclosure of intelligence budgets of CIA, NSA and 14
other intelligence agencies
Led by Rep. Welch
and Rep. Cynthia Lummis (R-WY), a bipartisan group of 62 Members of
Congress sent a letter to President Obama calling on him to disclose
the top-line budgets of the 16 federal agencies known to conduct
intelligence activities. These top-line budget figures are currently
considered classified information and remain out of the taxpayers
view.

Preserving
Vermonts rivers and tributaries for future
generations
Rep. Welch is calling on Congress to pass his legislation
designating the Upper Missisquoi and Trout Rivers in northern Vermont as part
