Congressman J. Randy Forbes (VA-04) met with Board of Supervisor Chairman Carson Tucker, County Administrator Pat Weiler, and the leadership and citizens of Powhatan County today to discuss a range of issues, from illegal immigration to rising healthcare costs and encouraging STEM education. Congressman Forbes also met with leadership and citizens in Amelia County to listen to concerns over the adverse impact of overreaching federal regulations are having on the local economy and communities.
