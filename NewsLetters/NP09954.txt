Congressman Lamborn's Fourth, Fifth, and
Sixth Amendments Added to the 2015 National Defense Authorization
Act
5/7/14
As part of today's all-day mark-up amendment
session for the 2015 National Defense Authorization Act, Congressman
Doug Lamborn's fourth, fifth, and sixth amendments were adopted into the
full 2015 National Defense Authorization Act.
4. Language requiring DOD to provide
a report looking at the technology to enable multi-aircraft control of
UAVs by February 15,
2015.
5. Language requesting a GAO review of DOD Arctic
Capabilities by February 6, 2015. This review is very important to US
Northern Command (NORTHCOM) based at Peterson AFB in Colorado
Springs.
6. Language
requiring the DOD to conduct a joint land use study on the Air Force
Academy, Peterson AFB, and Fort Carson to ensure that they continue to
enjoy good relationships with the surrounding community moving into the
future.
