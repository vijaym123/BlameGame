Congressman
Lamborn and House Armed Services Committee Vice Chairman Mac
Thornberry Discuss Local Military Installations with KVOR's Richard
Randall
8/26/14
This morning, prior to the beginning of a
comprehensive tour of Fort Carson, Congressmen Doug Lamborn (CO-05) and
Mac Thornberry (TX-13) conducted a joint interview with AM 740 KVOR
radio host Richard Randall. The topic of the interview was the future and
importance of the local military installations here in the Pikes Peak
region.
