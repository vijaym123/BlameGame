Walberg Votes for Border Security; Introduces Bill to Protect Property Rights
Weekly Update
Introduced H.R. 5212, the Civil Asset Forfeiture Reform Act, which seeks to restore personal property rights under the Fifth Amendment and ensure due process of law by reforming long-standing civil forfeiture policy
Helped pass the Veterans Access, Choice and Accountability Act, a bipartisan package to address problems within the VA and ensure veterans get the care they need
Voted for H.R. 5230 to provide funding to address our current crisis along the Southwest border
Attended an Oversight and Government Reform hearing which looked at how we can ensure abuses at the IRS never happen again
Went to the Monroe County Fair where I met many great people from the area and even a few animals
