Reminder: 2014 Congressional Art Competition
If you're a high school student in Ohio's 16th district, don't forget
to submit your artwork to the 2014 Congressional Art Competition. The
winning piece will hang in the U.S. Capitol for one year alongside
artwork from across the country. Deadline is April 4th. For the first
time this year, our office will award the People's Choice award
following a competition on my Facebook Page. Learn more about the 2014
Congressional Art Competition HERE
.

Weekly Column: The Establishing Beneficiary Equity In
The Hospital Readmission Program Act: Providing Relief For Our
Hospitals That Treat Our Nation's Most Vulnerable Patients
As a former businessman with a long record of experience in the health
care industry, it is clear that the Hospital Readmission Reduction
Program (HRRP) unfairly penalizes hospitals, including many in and
around Ohio's 16th district, caring for our country's most
disadvantaged population. Read more HERE
.
