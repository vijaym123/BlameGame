TONIGHT: House
Veterans Affairs Committee Oversight Hearing on Delays in
Care
6/23/14
Tonight at 5:30pm MT, the House Veterans Affairs
Committee (where Congressman Doug Lamborn sits as the second-ranking
Republican) will be holding a hearing examining the delays in crisis
care response from the VA. The hearing will also discuss the VA's
ability to provide more timely and easily accessible care to our
veterans.
Background
information from the House Veterans Affairs
Committee
As part of its new effort to provide
timelier access to care and prevent similar tragedies from occurring,
VA recently announced the Accelerating Access to Care Initiative.
This hearing will examine this initiative as well as the departments
overall capacity to provide timely, accessible and quality health care to
eligible veteran
patients.
