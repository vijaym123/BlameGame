Rep Walberg Votes for Balanced Budget; Holds IRS Official in Contempt of Congress
Weekly Update
Voted in favor of the fiscal year 2015 Budget to balance our federal budget in 10 years, reduce spending by $5.1 trillion, foster a healthy economy and create jobs.
Joined my fellow members on the Oversight &amp; Government Reform Committee in voting to hold former IRS official Lois Lerner in contempt of Congress for her refusal to give testimony on the IRSs targeting of Americans for political reasons
Participated in an Education &amp; the Workforce Committee mark-up to consider two bills that strengthen education through research and support charter schools
Examined government waste highlighted in the Government Accountability Offices 2014 report on duplicative federal programs
Participated in Education &amp; the Workforce Committee mark-up to approve legislation promoting employee privacy protection and workforce fairness
Held a Coffee Hour in Napoleon where residents were most concerned with the lack of American energy independence and the growth of the federal government
