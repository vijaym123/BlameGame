Staff Assistant - San Diego
Congresswoman Susan Daviss San Diego district office is searching for a full-time staff assistant.
Job responsibilities include fielding constituent calls, processing mail, coordinating the internship program, assisting with casework and constituent requests for information, maintaining a photographic database, drafting correspondence, maintaining office equipment, and other administrative duties.
In addition, the staff assistant occasionally represents Congresswoman Davis at community events.
This is an entry-level position.
Applicants must possess strong communication, organizational and writing skills.
Knowledge of the communities of the 53 rd
Congressional District is preferred.
APPLICATION INSTRUCTIONS:
Applicants should submit a letter of interest, rsum, and a list of at least three work references.
They should be submitted by email to:
employment.davis@mail.house.gov . You will receive an email confirmation when your application has been received.
No phone calls please.
