Fort Carson Receives Critical
Transportation Grant Funding
9/9/14
Today, the US
Department of Transportation awarded $1.2 million dollars to Fort Carson
in order to "develop conceptual design and preliminary engineering for
rehabilitation of access roads leading to Gate 19, which is planned
for reopening to provide alternate access to Ft. Carson's military
training infrastructure."
Congressman Doug Lamborn was a strong advocate of this grant request from Fort
Carson. Please see below for the Congressman's letter of
support.
