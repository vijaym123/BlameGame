Congressman Rice on "The Kelly File"
TONIGHT
Tune in to "The Kelly File" on Fox News tonight
at 9:00 PM ET
to watch Congressman Tom Rice
discuss
the legislative solution he introduced to rein in executive overreach,
the STOP Resolution.
Congressman Rice
will also discuss the House Judiciary Committee hearing at which he will
testify tomorrow.

overreaches and the Presidents constitutional duty to faithfully execute
the laws.
