Rep. Miller: Macomb Employees of General Dynamics Land Systems Helping to Protect Our Troops
Harrison Township, MI
&#8211; U.S. Representative Candice Miller (MI-10) visited the General Dynamics Land Systems facility located in Sterling Heights to learn about the collaborative efforts underway to enhance
combat vehicles used by U.S. troops throughout the world.
&#8220;Improving the capability and durability of combat vehicles relied on by our troops worldwide is a task that the nearly two thousand Land Systems employees in Macomb County should be very proud of,&#8221;
Miller noted after visiting the Sterling Heights facility.
&#8220;Through innovation and collaboration, these skilled employees are helping to protect our troops in the field from the dangers of combat.&#8221;
General Dynamics Land Systems is a wholly owned subsidiary of General Dynamics Corp., one of the world&#8217;s largest defense contractors with over 90,000 employees worldwide. Land Systems is headquartered in Sterling
Heights, MI &#8211; the nation&#8217;s defense vehicle industrial corridor &#8211; and has over 1,800 employees in its three Macomb County locations.
