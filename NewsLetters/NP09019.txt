Joint Round Table on EPA Water Rule TODAY at 10:00 a.m.
Dear Friend,
This morning I will be hosting a joint roundtable with my House
colleagues on the proposed EPA water rule to expand the definition of
"navigable waters of the United States". This new expansion of EPA
control has the potential to cause serious harm to businesses and
consumers.
This event will take place at the Arizona State Capitol from 10:00 a.m.
to 1:00 p.m. Participants in the Roundtable will be hosting a press
conference in Conference Room A immediately following the roundtable,
at 1:00 p.m.
If you cannot attend the hearing in person, it will be streaming live
online HERE
.
This roundtable is OPEN TO THE PUBLIC.
When:
Monday, June 2, 2014 from 10:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m.
Where:
Arizona State Capitol
Senate Hearing Room 1
1700 W Washington St
Phoenix, AZ 85007
Download the press packet here for more details.
As always, you can follow everything I am working on in Arizona and
Washington, D.C. through my website (http://gosar.house.gov
) on Twitter @repgosar
, or through Facebook
at Representative Paul Gosar.
Sincerely,
Paul A. Gosar, D.D.S.
Member of Congress
