Weekly Update
The president signed my bipartisan bill to increase efficiency within the federal government
Voted in favor for a series of bills that grow domestic energy production and promote American jobs
Introduced a bill to rename the downtown Jackson post office after
Officer James Bonneau , a Jackson police officer who was killed on duty in 2010 and subsequently awarded the Law Enforcement Congressional Badge of Bravery
Attended a House Education &amp; Workforce Committee hearing where I highlighted the great efforts and successes of the Jackson Area Career Center and the Monroe County ISDs Middle College are having in working with employers to prepare our young people with the skills needed in the current and future workforce
Visited Monroe County Middle College where I gave an update on D.C. and took questions from the students
Met with high school students from Dundee and Monroe as part of their DC Close Up trip to educate students to become active citizens in our democracy
