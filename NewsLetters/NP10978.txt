Weekly Update
House passed H.R. 2575, the Save American Workers (SAW) Act, legislation I introduced with Representatives Todd Young (IN), Pete Olson (TX) and Mike Kelly (PA) to repeal the 30-hour definition of full-time employment under the Presidents health care law and replace it with the traditional 40-hour definition to prevent reduced hours and wages for millions of hourly workers
Examined the failures of several states to properly set up health insurance exchange and its effect on taxpayers and consumers at an Oversight &amp; Government Reform Committee hearing
Attended Education and the Workforce hearing to examine how more men and women over the age of 25 are returning to school and how we can best meet their needs by modernizing educational practices
Spoke to Saline High School AP Government students about my legislative work and toured the schools award-winning auto shop
Surprised Saline student Ben Herrick with a document certifying his appointment to the U.S. Air Force Academy
