LEGISLATIVE ALERT: Congressman Lamborn Manages Floor Time
for Colorado Springs VA Clinic Naming
Bill
6/17/14
Today at
approximately 1:45pm MT/3:45pm ET, Congressman Doug Lamborn will be managing
time on the House Floor. Under consideration will be H.R. 3375, the
Congressman's bill to name the new Colorado Springs VA Clinic after PFC
Floyd K. Lindstrom. PFC Lindstrom was a Colorado Springs native who
gave his life serving the country in World War II and is a Medal of Honor
recipient.
Please
follow the action live on C-SPAN or at
www.cspan.org
