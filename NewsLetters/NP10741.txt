4/9/14 Update on Cruz/Lamborn
Anti-Terrorism Legislation
On Monday, April 7th, the Senate passed legislation from Senator
Ted Cruz designed to give the President the ability to deny diplomatic
visas to known international terrorists. Congressman Doug Lamborn has
introduced and is carrying the corresponding legislation in the House.
This morning, Congressman Lamborn sent the following letter to House
Leadership urging them to immediately bring the Cruz/Lamborn
anti-terrorism bill to the House Floor for a vote.
After receiving this letter, House Leadership has
agreed to the Congressman's request and the legislation will be up for a
final passage vote in the House on
Thursday, April 10th at
approximately 1:00pm ET/11:00am MT.
The Congressman's office will be keeping you posted on
the House progress of this legislation throughout the
week.
