November 14, 2014
Dear David,
The time to renew or sign up for health coverage for the upcoming year is fast approaching.
Open enrollment for 2015 health insurance plans begins November 15, 2014 and ends February 15, 2015. This is your opportunity to enroll in a Covered California health plan or make changes to your current plan.
Please read the information below carefully and take note of the deadlines listed:
Key Dates for 2015 Health Coverage Enrollment
Individuals who currently have health coverage:
If you are happy with your current Covered California health plan and do not wish to make changes, you will automatically be re-enrolled for coverage. However, please take some time to review your plan to make sure that coverage options and prices have not changed.
For those who wish to make changes to their current health plan, here are important dates to remember:
November 15, 2014:
First day to make changes to your coverage. Please visit
coveredca.com
to compare health plans.
December 15, 2014:
Enroll by this date to avoid a lapse in coverage and have your new health plan begin on January 1, 2015.
December 31, 2014:
Coverage under 2014 health plans end on this date.
Individuals without health coverage:
November 15, 2014:
First day to sign-up for health insurance. Contact Covered California to compare health plan options. Information is available at
www.coveredca.com
or by calling (800) 300-1506.
December 15, 2014:
You must enroll in a health plan and provide all supporting documentation by this date if you want coverage to begin on January 1, 2015.
February 15, 2015:
Last day to enroll in health coverage for 2015.
If you have any questions about open enrollment, please contact my office at 562-860-5050. My staff is available to answer your questions.
