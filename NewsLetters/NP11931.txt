February 26, 2014
As part of a larger effort to improve government transparency and keep the constituents of Pennsylvania's 4th Congressional District informed of current legislative business, I send out weekly updates at the beginning of each week that Congress is in session. These updates are a brief overview of upcoming legislative matters to be addressed each week. Please click on my photo below to listen to the provided audio clip.
As always, if you have any other questions or concerns on any issue or need help with a federal agency, please contact one of my offices anytime.
CLICK ON PICTURE TO PLAY SHORT AUDIO
H.R. 1211
- FOIA Oversight and Implementation Act of 2014 - would provide for greater public access to information (Sponsored by Rep. Darrell Issa / Oversight and Government Reform Committee)
H.R. 1232
- The Federal Information Technology Acquisition Reform Act - would eliminate duplication and waste in information technology acquisition and management (Sponsored by Rep. Darrell Issa / Oversight and Government Reform Committee)
H.R. 1423
- Taxpayers Right-to-Know Act - would provide taxpayers with an annual report disclosing the cost and performance of Government programs and areas of duplication among them (Sponsored by Rep. James Lankford / Oversight and Government Reform Committee)
H.R. 1123
- Unlocking Consumer Choice and Wireless Competition Act - would promote consumer choice and wireless competition by permitting consumers to unlock mobile wireless devices (Sponsored by Rep. Bob Goodlatte / Judiciary Committee)
H.R. 1944
- Private Property Rights Protection Act - would protect private property rights (Sponsored by Rep. James Sensenbrenner / Judiciary Committee)
H.R. 2530
- Taxpayer Transparency and Efficient Audit Act - would improve transparency and efficiency with respect to audits and communications between taxpayers and the Internal Revenue Service (Sponsored by Rep. Peter Roskam / Ways and Means Committee)
H.R. 2531
- Protecting Taxpayers from Intrusive IRS Requests Act - would prohibit the Internal Revenue Service from asking taxpayers questions regarding religious, political, or social beliefs (Sponsored by Rep. Peter Roskam / Ways and Means Committee)
H.R. 3308
- Taxpayer Transparency Act of 2014 - would require a Federal agency to include language in certain educational and advertising materials indicating that such materials are produced and disseminated at taxpayer expense (Sponsored by Rep. Billy Long / Oversight and Government Reform Committee)
H.R. 3865
- Stop Targeting of Political Beliefs by the IRS Act of 2014 - would prohibit the Internal Revenue Service from modifying the standard for determining whether an organization is operated exclusively for the promotion of social welfare for purposes of section 501(c)(4) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 (Sponsored by Rep. Dave Camp / Ways and Means Committee)
H.R. 3193
- Consumer Financial Protection and Soundness Improvement Act of 2013 - would strengthen the review authority of the Financial Stability Oversight Council of regulations issued by the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Sponsored by Rep. Sean Duffy / Financial Services Committee)
H.R. 2804
- All Economic Regulations Are Transparent Act of 2014 - would require the Administrator of the Office of Information and Regulatory Affairs to publish information about rules on the Internet (Sponsored by Rep. George Holding / Judiciary Committee)
H.R. 899
- Unfunded Mandates Information and Transparency Act of 2013 - would provide for additional safeguards with respect to imposing Federal mandates (Sponsored by Rep. Virginia Foxx / Oversight and Government Reform Committee / Budget Committee / Rules Committee / Judiciary Committee)
