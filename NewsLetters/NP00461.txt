
News

June 2, 2015
As part of a larger effort to improve government transparency and keep the constituents of Pennsylvania's 4th Congressional District informed of current legislative business, I send out updates at the beginning of each Session week that review legislative matters to be addressed. Please click on my photo below to listen to the audio clip.
As always, if you have questions or concerns on any issue or need help with a federal agency, please contact our offices anytime.
CLICK ON PICTURE TO PLAY SHORT AUDIO
H.R. 404
authorizes early repayment of obligations to the Bureau of Reclamation within the Northport Irrigation District in the State of Nebraska (Sponsored by Rep. Adrian Smith / Natural Resources Committee)
H.R. 533
revokes the charter of incorporation of the Miami Tribe of Oklahoma at the request of that tribe (Sponsored by Rep. Markwayne Mullin / Natural Resources Committee)
H.R. 1168
Native American Childrens Safety Act (Sponsored by Rep. Kevin Cramer / Natural Resources Committee)
H.R. 979
designates a mountain in the John Muir Wilderness of the Sierra National Forest as Sky Point (Sponsored by Rep. Tom McClintock / Natural Resources Committee)
H.R. 336
directs the Administrator of General Services, on behalf of the Archivist of the United States, to convey certain Federal property located in the State of Alaska to the Municipality of Anchorage, Alaska (Sponsored by Rep. Don Young / Transportation and Infrastructure Committee)
H.R. 944
reauthorizes the National Estuary Program, and for other purposes (Sponsored by Rep. Frank LoBiondo / Transportation and Infrastructure Committee)
S. 802
"Girls Count" Act of 2015 (Sponsored by Sen. Marco Rubio / Foreign Affairs Committee)
H.R. 1493
Protect and Preserve International Cultural Property Act (Sponsored by Rep. Eliot Engel / Foreign Affairs Committee)
H.R. 1335
Strengthening Fishing Communities and Increasing Flexibility in Fisheries Management Act (Sponsored by Rep. Don Young / Natural Resources Committee)
H.R. 2578
Commerce, Justice, Science, and Related Agencies Appropriations Act, 2016 (Sponsored by Rep. John Culberson / Appropriations Committee)
- Possible consideration of H.R. 2577
Transportation, Housing and Urban Development, and Related Agencies Appropriations Act, 2016 (Sponsored by Rep. Mario Diaz-Balart / Appropriations Committee)
- Possible Consideration of Legislation related to expiring provisions of the PATRIOT Act
