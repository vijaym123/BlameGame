If you haven't yet had the opportunity to see the new

the ir

here .
Apply to volunteer at the Paradise
Valley Hospital.
Volunteers get great perks like free
parking, free uniforms, meal tickets and free tuberculosis and skin
cell testing. Volunteers dedicate at least one four hour shift per week
for at least six months. There is a wide variety of volunteer
opportunities available, including with and without patients, customer service
or medical and the hours are flexible.
For
more information on how to volunteer, click
here .
