As a dad to two little girls, I recognize that fathers play a unique and important role in the lives of our children. As we observe Fathers Day, our Nation honors fatherhood and urges dads to commit themselves selflessly to the success and well-being of their children.

So today, please take a moment to think about the most important job many of us will ever have
being a Dad. Happy Fathers Day to all of you
have a great day and enjoy the blessing of your family.
Sincerely,
Scott G. Perry
