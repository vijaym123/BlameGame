A Conversation on Health Care Reform in California
Dear Friend,
Have questions about the Affordable Care Act, CoveredCalifornia
, or any of the other aspects of health care reform in
California? Then join me for a Crowdhall, in conjunction with Covered
California, from now until Friday. Crowdhall is an online town hall,
where you can ask us your questions, or vote for your favorite
questions, and get the answers you need.
Click HERE
to start asking us questions right now, or visit:
http://c.hall.co/h/143
It is always a pleasure to host discussions with my constituents.
Please feel free to reach out to me by email, Facebook, Google Plus, or
Twitter at any time.
Sincerely,
