ANNUAL ARTS COMPETITION WINNERS: I'm pleased to announce nearly $30,000 in arts scholarships was awarded to four deserving students on Saturday during my 2015 Congressional Arts Competition at the Lou Walker Senior Center. Since 2007, our office - in conjunction with our excellent higher education partners - has awarded more than $280,000 in arts scholarships to Fourth District students. For more, click HERE .
PUBLIC SERVICE RECOGNITION WEEK: May 3rd through 9th is Public Service Recognition Week to honor federal, state, county and local government employees and members of the uniformed services. My staff and I are grateful for the opportunity to thank you for all you do on behalf of our communities and our nation. Your hard work does not go unnoticed, and we want you to know that we are proud to serve with you. For more, click HERE .
PREPARING FOR HOMEOWNERSHIP: Bank of America is hosting an Atlanta Homeowner Assistance Event on Friday, May 1 and Saturday, May 2 at the Sheraton Atlanta on 165 Courtland Street to help potential home buyers with home financing and assist current homeowners facing financial difficulties. For more information, click HERE .
