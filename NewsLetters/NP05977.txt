Dear Friend,
Are you planning a visit to Washington, D.C.? Whether you're coming on a short business trip or an extended family vacation, my office can help you make arrangements to see some of the most popular federal attractions in our nation's capital.
By filling out
this form , my office can help you book tours to the White House, U.S. Capitol, Pentagon, State Department, Supreme Court, Bureau of Engraving and Printing, and the Library of Congress. After submitting your tour request, you can expect to hear from my office within a couple of days.
These tours are an excellent way to see all that the nation's capital has to offer.
All of these tours are free of charge. Tickets are provided to 7th Congressional District constituents on a first-come, first-served basis. Due to the large number of visitors, please place your request as early as possible to maximize your chances.
Of course, there are many other places in Washington, D.C. that you and your family may be interested in visiting. The links below have information on other popular sites located throughout the capital region.
