Deadline to signup for health insurance
is
February 15!
Open enrollment ends
February
15 .
If you want to
sign up for health care coverage for 2015, be sure to visit
healthcare.gov and sign up for a plan by
February 15 !
Through the
Federal Marketplace,
you can sign up for an affordable plan that fits your
budget and health needs. Dont miss out on this important opportunity
to get covered.
After February 15, the Federal Marketplace will close until the 2016
open enrollment season begins.
Anyone who is not covered in 2015
could face penalties when they file their tax
return.
If you have family or friends that do
not have health insurance, encourage them to sign up for coverage
before they miss this important deadline.
If you have any questions about enrolling for
health insurance through the
Federal Marketplace ,
please call my Roseville office at (586) 498-7122 and my staff will be
happy to assist you.
