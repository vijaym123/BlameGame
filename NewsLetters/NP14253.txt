November 12, 2013
Dear
Friend,
I would like to invite you to the 2013 Rangel Career Fair on Tuesday,
November 12. The Fair will run from 10:00 am to 2:00 pm at the City
College of New York, Great Shepard Hall, located at 160 Convent Ave at
138th St. This event is free and open to the
public.
Over 60 different organizations with job openings will
be represented, including the New York City Police Department (NYPD),
the New York City Fire Department (NYFD), the U.S. Department of State,
the U.S. Secret Service, Columbia University, North American Power,
SiriusXM Satellite Radio, Aflac, and Time Warner Cable. There will also
be groups such as AARP WorkSearch and the Center for Employment
Opportunities there to offer human resource assistance and job
training.
My top priority is jobs, jobs, jobs. I am
constantly reaching out to agencies and companies to proovide you
opportunities to explore various careers and learn valuable skills. Please come
dressed professionally, with multiple copies of your
resume.
Please check out my website at
www.Rangel.house.gov . for more information. Don't forget
to like me on
Facebook
and follow me on
Twitter . It's always a great honor serving
you.
Best regards,
Charles Rangel
