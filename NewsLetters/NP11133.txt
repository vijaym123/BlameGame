March 25, 2014
Are you or
someone you know looking for a job?
For the past 19 years, Congressman Xavier Becerra has worked
with local elected officials, city, state and county agencies and the
Los Angeles Dodgers to connect job seekers in his district with
employment opportunities.
Approximately a hundred
employers and employment service providers
from around the Los
Angeles area will be gathered at the:
Saturday, March 29, 2014, 10 a.m. to 1 p.m.
Dodger
Stadium
1000 Elysian Park Avenue
Los Angeles, CA 90012
Free
parking available through Elysian Park Ave / Stadium Way
entrance
Download the official flyer
Job
applicants are encouraged to bring a resume and wear business attire
as
employers will be actively recruiting and conducting
on-site interviews.
Admission and parking
at the job fair are free.
A shuttle service will
also be available to transport attendees from the corner of Elysian
Park Avenue and Sunset Boulevard to the stadium entrance.
Need more
information?
Please call Congressman Becerra's office at 213-481-1425.

also visit

for more information.
