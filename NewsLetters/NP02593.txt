February 27, 2015
Moore Introduces Bill to Reauthorize Export-Import
Bank
I
introduced legislation
to reintroduce the Export-Import Bank
supported by Milwaukee business leaders
who
rely on the bank to stay competitive in the global
economy.
Urging the Supreme Court to Strike Down WIs Voter ID
Law
My
colleagues and I on the Congressional Black Caucus
sent an amicus brief to the Supreme Court
advocating the overturn
of Wisconsins unconstitutional and discriminatory voter ID
law.
Now and Then: My Time as a VISTA AmeriCorps
Volunteer
To celebrate the 50 th
anniversary of AmeriCorps VISTA, I
was invited to
speak on my time
as a VISTA
volunteer in Milwaukee.
Recognizing Unsung Heroes for Black History
