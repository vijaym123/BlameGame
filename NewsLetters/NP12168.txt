My Office is Ready to Help You
Dear Friend,
As your voice in Congress, I am proud to work on your behalf in
Washington, D.C., and here at home in Philadelphia and Montgomery
County. Over the past nine years, my office has successfully assisted
more than 16,500 constituents with various matters related to federal
agencies. If you, a member of your family or someone you know needs
assistance with a federal agency, please be sure to contact my office
and we will do everything we can to assist you in a timely and
effective manner.
Each day, my district offices in Northeast Philadelphia and Abington
assist constituents with a host of matters, including:
*	Resources for those applying for Medicare, retirement benefits,
disability benefits or spousal benefits.
*	Determining the status of Social Security claims that have been
pending longer than the agency's designated time frame.
*	Obtaining or expediting passport applications.
*	Checking the status of applications that have been pending with
U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services or the State Department
longer than the allotted time frame.
*	Resources for those seeking health coverage.
*	Resources for those with questions or issues regarding the
Internal Revenue Service.
*	Information on federal student loan options or other forms of
financial aid.
*	Small business resources and business opportunities with the
federal government.
*	Guidance with Veterans Administration hospital issues, claims
for compensation or pensions, replacement of medals and other veterans'
services.
Please be sure to visit my website
and Facebook
page for how I can best serve you, and do
not hesitate to call or stop by my offices in Philadelphia
(215-335-3355) or Abington (215-517-6572) if you have questions or
concerns.
Sincerely,
Allyson Y. Schwartz
Member of Congress
P.S. -
If you would like stay updated with the latest information, be
sure to subscribe to my eNewsletter
.
