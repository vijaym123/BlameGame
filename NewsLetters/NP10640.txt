Friends,
I would like to invite you, as a resident of the Third District of
Michigan, to town halls
I am
hosting in Caledonia and Marshall on Monday, April 14, and Tuesday,
April 15. I will discuss matters under consideration in Congress, and I
look forward to hearing from you and answering your questions.
The Caledonia town hall will be held from 5:30 p.m. to 6:30 p.m. on
Monday, April 14, at the Caledonia Township Office at 8196 Broadmoor
Avenue SE
.
The Marshall town hall will be held from 5:00 p.m. to 6:00 p.m. on
Tuesday, April 15, at the Calhoun County Building at 315 West Green
Street
.
These town halls are open to the public. Please contact my Grand Rapids
office at (616) 451-8383 or my Battle Creek office at (269) 205-3823 if
you have any further questions.
Sincerely,
Justin Amash
Member of Congress
