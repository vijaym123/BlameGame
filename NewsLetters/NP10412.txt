Westmoreland Hosts Social Security and Medicare
Information Workshop
On Thursday, April 24, 2014, I
will host a Social Security and Medicare Information Workshop at the
Asa Powell Expo Center in Newnan from 9:00am
12:00pm. There will be
representatives from the Social Security Administration and the Area
Agency on Aging to discuss the opportunities and services they provide,
as well as an opportunity to speak about your case with them and with
my staff.
I would like to thank
the speakers for giving their time to help the constituents of
Georgias Third District in getting the information that they need and my
staff for arranging this event. This workshop is an opportunity to get
more information on the services out there and ask local experts your
questions to make sure youre getting access to your
benefits.
The Asa Powell Expo Center is located at
197 Temple Avenue (behind the Waffle House) in Newnan, Georgia. For
more information, contact my Newnan office at 770-683-2033 or visit
my
website .

Capitol Corner: House Committee Holds Lerner in
Contempt
Almost a year ago the American people
learned an ugly truth about an agency they already feared: the Internal
Revenue Service (IRS). News broke that the IRS was targeting specific
tax-exempt organizations, specifically those whose names involved
patriot or conservative. What we dont know is why or who ordered the
investigation. And a year later, the American people still dont have all
the answers.
The House did not
take these accusations lightly and acted quickly, calling in employees
and issuing subpoenas for documents and testimonies to get to the
bottom of this unjust and prejudice auditing. All roads appeared to lead
to Former IRS Exempt Organizations division Director Lois Lerner;
however, she has only stated her innocence and pled the Fifth every time
she has been called before the House Oversight committee to testify. The
investigation has found that Lerner had a large role in the targeting
scandal, and her refusal to comply or answer questions is a red
