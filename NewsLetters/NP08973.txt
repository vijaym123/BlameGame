Dear Friend,
Congressman John Micas work to bring common sense
and fiscal responsibility to Washington, garnered him top
recognition by the Council for Citizens Against Government
Waste.
The taxpayer watchdog organization President Tom Schatz said,
Your 2013 Congressional Ratings are a true testament of your
willingness to stand up for American taxpayers.
Rep. Mica said, With our expanding federal deficit,
it is vital that every Member of Congress concentrate on tackling
waste, fraud and abuse and make government more
efficient.
Citizens Against Government Waste is the nations largest nonpartisan,
nonprofit organization dedicated to eliminating waste, fraud, abuse and
mismanagement in government.
Congressman Mica has also
received of the Tax Fighter Award, the Defenders of Liberty
Award, Hero of the Taxpayer Award, The Defender of Economic
Freedom Award and also the Guardian of Small Business
Award.
