
The President has a Constitutional duty to execute our nations laws
period. No picking and choosing. Before the House Judiciary Committee last week was legislation I cosponsored, which would allow the House or Senate to authorize a lawsuit against the Executive Branch for failure to faithfully execute the laws, restoring balance of powers and reining in the growing problem of executive overreach.
