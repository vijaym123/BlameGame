Dear Friends,
My office is hosting an informational session for residents of the 34th District of California to learn more about the recent executive actions on immigration taken by President Obama. These programs include Expanded Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals (DACA) and Deferred Action for Parental Accountability (DAPA).
Immigration Information Session on DACA and DAPA Saturday, January 24th, 2015
Laborers' Local 300 Hall
2005 West Pico Boulevard
Los Angeles, CA
90006
Parking will be available off of Westlake Avenue
You're invited
to attend this session with special guest Susan Curda, District Director for US Citizenship and Immigration Services in District 23 (Los Angeles).
When you attend, you will:
- Learn about President Obama's executive Action
- Learn about general eligibility and criteria for expanded DACA and DAPA
- Have the opportunity for your questions to be answered
- One-on-one counseling will be available - if interested, please call my Los Angeles Office at 213-481-1425 to RSVP.
