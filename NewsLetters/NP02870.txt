Budget Hearing at the Veterans Affairs
Committee
2/11/15
Today, Congressman Doug Lamborn
participated in a budget hearing in the House Veterans Affairs Committee.
The Committee heard from a distinguished panel designed to discuss the
depth and breadth of the budget proposals coming from the VA
Department.
