Wisdom of the District: Your Health Care
The Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act (PPACA) will continue to roll out in 2014 and beyond.
We want to hear how the PPACA has affected the families in Hampton Roads and on Virginias Eastern Shore. Please select one or more of the options below, and elaborate on your story in the space provided.

( ) My employer cut my hours so it doesn't have to provide health care.
( ) My spouses employer dropped me from its plan.
