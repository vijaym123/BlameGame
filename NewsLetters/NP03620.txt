Christmas 2014
Statement
12/24/14
To
celebrate Christmas, Congressman Doug Lamborn has released the following
statement:
This Christmas
season, I wish to send you and your family warm wishes for a very
Merry Christmas. As we celebrate the birth of our Lord and Savior Jesus
Christ, we are mindful and thankful for the blessings God has bestowed
on our nation and our region. Please enjoy this restful time with loved
ones and take a few moments to contemplate the hope, love, and joy
that surround us all during this special time of
year.
