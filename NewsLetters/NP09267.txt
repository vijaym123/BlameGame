CONTACT ME
May 2014
Dear Neighbors,
Please join me on Thursday, June 5th for a Hiring Event I am hosting. I
know how valuable meeting face-to-face with potential employers is, and
I am confident that this Hiring Event will offer many exciting
opportunities to make connections, learn valuable strategies and
potentially find your next job.
You can pre-register and find more information on this Hiring Event
through this form
on my website. If you have any
questions or need any assistance, please contact my District Office at
(847) 793-0625.
I hope to see you on June 5th.
Thank you,
Brad Schneider
