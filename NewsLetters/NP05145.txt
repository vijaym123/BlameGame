October 24, 2014
SPECIAL EDITION SCALISE CAPITOL REPORT - HOW CAN WE SERVE YOU?
Hello,
Its a privilege to represent the First District of Louisiana in Congress. I am happy to help you in any way we can. Ive compiled a list of a few resources and tools below that I hope you find helpful. As always, please reach out to my office if you have any questions and let us know how we can better serve you.
Veterans
Have you served our country and need assistance navigating the Department of Veterans Affairs or would like to know about any tools or resources available to you in LA-01?
Click here
or on the photo below.
2014 Veterans Job Fair- Veterans deserve great career options, and this year we had another successful Veterans Job Fair to connect veterans with local business leaders. More than 160 service members attended and more than 40 businesses had representatives on hand to accept resumes and interview applicants.
Click here
to view more photos.
Flag Requests
The Capitol may be
under renovation , but we are still accepting your flag requests! If youre interested in having a flag flown over the U.S. Capitol to mark a notable birthday, anniversary or life event, fill out
this form
so that my staff can fly a flag just for you.

Click here

Service Academy Nominations
America's military service academies offer a unique opportunity for young leaders who are interested in pursuing a post-secondary education while also serving our nation at the same time.
Click here
for a nomination form.
Academy Day 2014 -Earlier this year, I co-hosted a successful Service Academy Day with Senator David Vitter and Congressman Cedric Richmond. The event brought together high school students and representatives from those service academies. More than 60 attendees joined us at Federal City in New Orleans to learn about the process to gain admission into our nation's service academies.
