Rep. Miller Joins Students at KUKA Systems North America to Celebrate National Manufacturing Day
Harrison Township, MI &#8211; Today, U.S. Representative Candice Miller (MI-10) joined Macomb County Executive Mark Hackel, Macomb Intermediate School District Superintendent Michael DeVault, and students from
Sterling Heights Stevenson
High School to tour KUKA Systems North America, LLC., located in Sterling Heights, Michigan, to celebrate National Manufacturing Day.
&#8220;Today, in celebration of National Manufacturing Day, I had the opportunity to join talented students from
Sterling Heights Stevenson
High School on a tour of KUKA Systems in Sterling Heights, where we learned about the cutting-edge technologies they employ to make sophisticated assembly robotics,&#8221;
Miller said after the tour.
&#8220;It was truly inspiring to visit this innovative manufacturing company which, like many others in here in Southeast Michigan, offers great opportunities for the next generation of ambitious workers.&#8221;
About KUKA Systems North America, LLC.:
KUKA Systems is an international supplier of engineering services and flexible automation systems, servicing the automotive, aerospace, alternative energy, and manufacturing industries. With over 4,000 employees worldwide, KUKA&#8217;s North
American division is based in Sterling Heights, Michigan and is responsible for their worldwide aerospace division. For more information, please visit them online:
http://www.kuka-robotics.com/usa/en/company/
