Give States a Choice on Obamacare
A
mother of three emailed us because her familys premiums increased $300 under
Obamacare, while their co-pays and deductibles doubled as well. If you like
your health insurance, you can't keep it. If you like your doctor, you can't
keep him or her either. And if you like your job, you may not be able to keep
it, or your hours.
This is why I joined our U.S. Senators Tim Scott and Lindsey Graham, and Rep. Mick Mulvaney to introduce legislation, which allows states to opt-out of the major mandates in Obamacare. Read more about our bill
here .
The value of work
Last week, I had the opportunity to tour Workability, a business in Inman, which provides jobs and job training to people with special needs or disabilities.
