Please join me for a meeting with my Farmers Advisory Board on Wednesday, February 19 th
at the Virginia Diner in Wakefield. On our agenda will be the reauthorization of the farm bill, the impact federal regulations have on the agricultural industry, pending trade agreements, and any other issues affecting our farmers that you would like to discuss.
Congressman Randy Forbes Farmers Advisory Board Meeting
Wednesday, February 19 th
9:00 a.m. - 10:15 a.m.
The Virginia Diner
408 County Drive North, Wakefield
My Farmers Advisory Board is comprised of farmers who advise me on a wide range of issues impacting the agricultural community in the 4th Congressional District of Virginia. We use these meetings as working sessions where we share ideas and common sense solutions to address current challenges. I hope you can join us and look forward to seeing you on the 19th.
Please feel free to invite someone to come with you.
To RSVP for this event and/or to join my Farmers Advisory Board, please contact Mr. Ron White, District Director at
Ronald.White@mail.house.gov
or (804) 318-1363.
Yours in Service,
Randy Forbes
Member of Congress
