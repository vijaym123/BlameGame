David,
Voting has begun for the 2014 Congressional Art Competition!
And now it's your chance to help decide who wins this year's
competition from all the great submissions my office received.
Click here to vote for your favorite student artwork online!
Vote for your favorite student art work today!
The Congressional Art Competition, An Artistic Discovery, is an annual
event that allows high school students from eastern Iowa and across the
country to showcase their artistic abilities. High school students from
the Iowa's First Congressional District have submitted their artwork to
my office, and their work is now displayed in an online gallery on my
website.
The winner of the competition will win two round-trip airline tickets
to Washington, D.C. In addition, the winner's artwork will be displayed
in the U.S. Capitol for a year.
Click to vote for your favorite artwork today.
Online voting closes Wednesday, April 30 and the winner will be
announced shortly after. Good luck to all the students who submitted
art this year.
All the best,
Bruce
P.S. Feel free to forward this email and urge your friends and family
to vote for their favorite, too!
