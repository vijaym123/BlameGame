Career Reentry and Development Panel
Dear Friend,
If you have considered developing your skills to transition to a new career or are reentering the workforce after time away, I encourage you to join me at an upcoming panel discussion.
Participants will hear from local experts who will give an overview of opportunities in San Diego for people interested in becoming more competitive in todays economy.
Expect to hear from experts on apprenticeship opportunities, programs offered through community colleges, extended studies programs, and an overview of the local labor market. There will be plenty of time for Q&amp;A.
