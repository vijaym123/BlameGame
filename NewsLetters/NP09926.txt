My amendment to save military commissaries passed the House of Representatives
Recently, I have been hearing from local Veterans and active duty service members about their concerns with the proposed DoD budget, including the proposed cuts to commissaries.
I recently introduced an amendment that would save these commissaries . My amendment passed the House April 30, 2014.
Introduced Bipartisan Bill to Encourage Private Flood Insurance Market
Rep. Murphy (D-Palm Beach Gardens) and I introduced a
bipartisan flood insurance bill
in the House of Representatives that would allow states to be able to fully utilize their regulatory framework (like the one the Florida Legislature just approved) to license flood insurance policies.
Select Committee to Investigate Benghazi
Speaker Boehner intends to hold a vote to create a new
select committee to investigate the September 11, 2012 terrorist attack on the U.S. consulate in Benghazi , Libya in which four Americans were killed. For more than a year I have been calling for a select committee to investigate and report on the attack. We demand answers and we demand justice.
