November 5, 2013
Join Me at a Town Hall
Dear Friend,
I am pleased to announce an upcoming town hall meeting. I hope that you can join me for a discussion about Washington overreach and our federal spending problem. More importantly, I look forward to hearing and discussing your ideas and suggestions.
Thursday, November 7th
6:00 - 7:00 pm
Spring Branch Middle School Auditorium
1000 Piney Point Road
Houston, TX 77024
The school entrance is on Piney Point, and parking will be available in front of the school. Should you need further assistance, please do not hesitate to call my Houston office at (713) 682-8828. I look forward to talking with you on Thursday.
Sincerely,
John Culberson
