METROPLEX-DEADLINE TO GET COVERED IS FEBRUARY 15 TH
Dear Friends,
Are you and your family covered for all your healthcare needs? If you need to renew or enroll for quality affordable healthcare, your deadline is February 15.
Remember, there are 3 ways to enroll:
1)
Online,
by going to
HealthCare.gov .
2)
By phone,
by calling the Marketplace Call Center at 1-800-318-2596.
3)
In person,
by contacting a Navigator, a Certified Application Counselor, or a licensed Agent or Broker.
Certified Navigators are readily available for residents of the Metroplex to receive information and assistance on enrolling for healthcare insurance. To find a navigator near you, visit:
localhelp.healthcare.gov
or call 1-800-318-2596.
Have General Questions About the Affordable Care Act?
Visit HealthCare.Gov
or call
1-800-318-2596,
24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
