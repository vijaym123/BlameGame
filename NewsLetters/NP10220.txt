News from Representative John Delaney
2014 U.S. Service Academy Information Forum
On May 22, 2014, Congressman John K. Delaney will host his annual U.S.
Service Academy Information Forum with representatives from the U.S.
Air Force Academy, the U.S. Coast Guard Academy, the U.S. Merchant
Marine Academy, the U.S. Military Academy, and the U.S. Naval Academy
for classes entering July 2015. All interested 9th, 10th, 11th, and
12th grade students and their parents are invited to attend and learn
about the academies and their admission requirements.
Note: RSVPs are appreciated but not required.
Frederick Forum
Thursday, May 22, 2014
6:00 pm - 7:30 pm
The National Museum of Civil War Medicine
2nd Floor Conference Room
48 East Patrick Street
Frederick, MD 21705
Parking garage located next door.
For more information or to RSVP, please contact
Congressman Delaney's District Office at 301-733-2900.
For updates from our office
like us on Facebook
and follow us on Twitter.
