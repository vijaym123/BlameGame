Have You Filled Out My Survey?
Dear Friend,
My team and I always want to hear from the people of the Sixth
Congressional District of Kentucky. We are currently finishing up our
Constituent Survey, which asks for your views on a number of important
issues. Thank you to those of who have already taken the time to
respond and share your views with me.
If you have not yet filled out our Constituent Survey, I encourage you
to do so. The survey can be filled out below or you can fill it out on
my website by visiting http://barr.house.gov/constituent-survey
.
I want to make sure you know about my upcoming events and office hours
in your area, so please let me know how best to share that information
