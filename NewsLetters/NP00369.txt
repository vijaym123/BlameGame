New Interactive Website for Georgia's 11th District Unveiled
We are proud to announce the unveiling of a new interactive website designed specifically with you in mind. Whether you're trying to juggle the demands of a busy life or meet the needs of your growing family, this website makes it easy to find what you're looking for and takes the stress out of contacting your Congressional office.
With just the click of a button, you can request to open a case with a federal agency, reach out to me or a member of my staff, or watch a video on a hot-button topic being discussed in the halls of Congress. Whatever your needs, there's something for you. We hope you enjoy looking around!
Website Features :
Easy to navigate design
Social media links
Breaking News and Videos
E-Newsletter Subscription Sign-Up
11th District Highlights and Information
Up-to-date Voting Record
