<![endif] Caring
for our Veterans at the Bad Axe VA Community-Based Outpatient Clinic
Rep. Miller stopped in at the Bad Axe Veterans Affairs Community-Based Outpatient Clinic and met with the clinics health care service providers.
Since the clinics opening in October 2010, the Bad Axe VA Clinic has provided various mental health services, including counseling for Post Traumatic Stress Disorder, physical therapy, womens health, as well as primary care services
to more than 4,000 Veterans residing in Huron County.
