Staff Changes in Local Colorado Springs
Office
4/2/15
Congressman Doug Lamborn is pleased to
announce two staff changes in his Colorado Springs District
Office.
Jarred Rego is now serving as the
District Director. In this capacity, Jarred will be managing the
Congressman's District Office and overseeing constituent service and
outreach efforts. He will also serve as the lead representative for the
Congressman when he is in Washington DC. Jarred is in his second stint with
the Congressman, following service as District Press Secretary and
tenure as Colorado Springs Mayor Steve Bach's Senior Communications
Specialist. Jarred will continue to serve in his capacity as
Communications Director for the Congressman, a position he has held since December
of 2013.
If you would like to connect with Jarred, he
can be reached at jarred.rego@mail.house.gov or at (719)
377-0446.
Linda Rutzen has joined the
District Office as Office Administrator. Linda joins the team from the
City of Colorado Springs Communications Department. An experienced,
dedicated, and detail-oriented administrative professional, Linda will
be the friendly face who first greets you at the Colorado Springs
office.
"Thank you to both of these
talented individuals for their tireless efforts and their willingness
to serve the great people of our region. Please join me in welcoming
them to their new roles and I look forward to working with each of them
to provide excellent customer service to our
District."
- Congressman Doug Lamborn
(CO-05)
