Dear Friends,
Do you or anyone you know need healthcare coverage?
I f so, I urge you to
learn more about the Affordable Care Act by joining me at the Brownsville Events Centeron Monday, November 24, 2014 from 9 AM to 4 PM for aHealth Care Enrollment Fair.It is an honor to announce that
Julian Castro,
Secretary of the U .
S .
Department of Housing and Urban Development
(HUD),
will serve as the guest speaker.
During the fair, representatives from Get Covered America will give presentations and answer questions concerning healthcare enrollment under the Affordable Care Act. Additionally, Certified Application Counselors from Cognosante, VAIL, Brownsville Community Health Center, Su Clinica Familiar and Migrant Health Plans Salud will be available to assist with enrollment.
It is my hope that the people of South Texas will take advantage of this great opportunity and gain access to
healthc are. If you, a family member or friend is without health insurance, I encourage you to come out and attend this free event.
Those wishing to enroll should bring proof of income, proof of residence, their Social Security Number, and a photo I.D.
