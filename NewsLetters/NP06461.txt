
September 12, 2014
SHARE ON:
Walberg Works to Rein in Excessive Government
Weekly Update
Passed a House resolution condemning the Presidents failure to comply with the law and notify Congress before releasing five Taliban terrorists in May from Guantanamo Bay, Cuba
Voted in favor of H.R. 5078, the Waters of the United States Regulatory Overreach Protection Act, to uphold the successful federal-state partnership in place for 40 years and prevent the Environmental Protection Agency and Army Corps of Engineers from expanding federal regulations to nearly all bodies of water without Congressional approval
Joined my colleagues in passing H.R. 3522, the Employee Health Care Protection Act, which would allow Americans in the group health insurance market to keep or choose the plans they like
Questioned three Inspector Generals at an Oversight and Government Reform Committee hearing after they testified how federal agencies refused to supply key documents which is impeding their ability to complete investigations and costing taxpayers dollars
Spoke on the House floor about the Civil Asset Forfeiture Reform Act I introduced to limit the scope, power and reach of the government
Participated in the USO Care Package Service Project to assemble packages for our nation's brave servicemen and women.
