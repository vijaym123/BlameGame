How Can I Better Help You?
Dear Friends,
As your representative, my top priority is to be of service to the constituents living in my district.
My offices in Washington, DC and across the 28 th
District of Texas work hard every day to provide quality and accessible constituent services to you. One of my most important responsibilities is to help constituents interact with the many agencies and offices of the federal government.
My staff can help with matters involving:
-Medicare
-Veteran's Affairs
-Passport Services, Immigration
-Social Security
-Applying for a federal grant
-Applying to a United States Service Academy (high school students)
-Requesting a flag to be flown over the U.S. Capitol
-Organizing tours of the White House, U.S. Capitol, and other Washington, D.C. landmarks**
We work hard to provide top-level service and we want your feedback.
