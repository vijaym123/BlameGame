Senator Johnson's Office Announces June Dairy Month Instagram
Photo
Contest
In honor of June Dairy Month, Senator Johnson has launched
an Instagram photo contest. Wisconsinites are encouraged to take a
picture of a dairy farm in their area and to share it on the senators
Instagram account using the hashtag #AdventuresInDairyland. The
contestants with the most likes will get their photos displayed on the
senators Instagram and Facebook pages and one lucky photographer will get
her or his photo featured on Senator Johnsons personal
desk.
