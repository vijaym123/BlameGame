Dear Friend,
As we enter the second session of the 113th Congress, there are many
ways that our office can assist you. Whether you need help with a
federal agency or if you are planning a trip to Washington, we can
help. Please sign up for our Facebook
, Twitter
, and YouTube
pages and subscribe to our weekly
e-newsletter
for updates from me here
.
You can find information regarding all of our constituent services on
our website
.
In addition to our office in Washington, DC, we have an office in
Wadsworth, as well as an office in Parma that will be opening soon.
Below you will find additional ways in which our office is here for you:
Book Tours In Washington
If you are planning a trip to Washington, we can help arrange a Capitol
tour, request a White House tour, or make recommendations on other
sites of interest around our nation's capitol. Find information here
or call
(330) 334-0040.
Request A Flag Flown Over The Capitol
If you are interested in having a flag flown over the U.S. Capitol
building, one can be purchased through our office. Find more
information here
.
Help With Federal Agencies
Our office can help you with finding out the status of a case,
requesting additional information, and requesting an expedited response
or decision from a government agency. Find information regarding
Medicare, Medicaid, and Social Security here
, and
information for military personnel here
.
Contact our Wadsworth office (330) 334-0040 for assistance.
Nominations For U.S. Service Academies
High school students who are interested in attending one of our nation's
service academies may request a nomination. Find more information here
.
Help With Federal Grants
Find information and resources regarding available government and
private funding for various programs here
Apply For An Internship
Interested in interning in one of our offices? Find information about
internship opportunities here
.
Find Information On Disaster Preparedness Here
Find information regarding disaster preparedness here
.
Find Information On Student Financial Aid
Looking for a student loan? Find information here
.
