This weekend Ill be joining Dr. Sanjay Gupta on CNN to discuss the bipartisan medical marijuana oil legislation I recently introduced to help children and adults suffering from epilepsy and other seizure disorders. You can watch the interview on CNN on Saturday around 4:30 p.m. or on Sunday around 7:30 a.m. If you cant tune in on TV, you can watch online at
http://sanjayguptamd.blogs.cnn.com/ .
If youd like to stay up to date on this topic or the other things Im working on, I encourage you to follow my social media accounts. By clicking on the Facebook, Twitter, YouTube, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Pinterest images on the right-hand side, you'll be able to follow me via social media, which will allow you to receive the most current information on activities in the 4th Congressional District and Washington, DC, while saving hard-earned tax dollars on postage and printing costs.
