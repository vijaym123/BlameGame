Dear Friend,
I'm honored to have the opportunity to serve as your Representative in
the U.S. Congress.
Since taking office in January, I've been listening
to the concerns and ideas of folks across the Fifth District
to ensure
that I can be an effective advocate in Washington.
I'd also like to hear from you.
Will you take a moment to fill out
this brief survey?
By giving me just a minute of your time, you can
help me focus on the needs and priorities of the 5th District and
better serve you in Congress.
To find out more about my work and stay in touch about events like
Congress on Your Corner, tele-town halls, and other activities I
frequently host to hear from constituents, please "Like" my Facebook
page
, and follow me on Twitter
.
