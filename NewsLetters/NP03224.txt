
Congresswoman Joyce Beatty
Invites you to attend an
Affordable Care Act (ACA) Informational Seminar
in conjunction with
The Small Business Administration (SBA) &amp;
The Internal Revenue Service (IRS)
To help small business owners better understand the new ACA, Congresswoman Joyce Beatty and Senator Sherrod Brown are bringing the SBA and the IRS to you. Small business owners will have the opportunity to hear from officials at both agencies and ask questions about its implementation.
As of January 1, 2015, regulatory changes have been made to the ACA that affect small businesses. Representatives will be available to explain these changes.
